# Mascot Film Asset Bible Seed

User direction: favor assets like anime-mascot storytelling sheets, character bibles, and storyboard packs that are detailed enough to support filmmaking, motion generation, and editing workflows rather than generic standalone art.

## Priority Users

- Indie filmmakers building short films from generated assets.
- Storyboard artists and previsualization teams.
- Animation directors and motion-design editors.
- Game-cinematic creators building cutscenes from still-image reference.
- Agents that need structured retrieval over character, shot, prop, and sequence metadata.

## Desired Asset Families

- Character turnaround sheet with front, side, back, and three-quarter views.
- Expression and reaction pack with readable pose silhouettes.
- Species or creature dossier with anatomy, scale, habits, and prop relations.
- Costume and prop plate with repeated detail closeups.
- Storyboard beat page for one short sequence.
- Camera blocking board with shot size, angle, move, and subject relation.
- Animation timing or pose ladder for one recurring action.
- Edit-planning board for GPT image to Seedance motion to CapCut assembly.
- Environment continuity sheet that fixes recurring landmarks, props, weather, and time of day.
- Scene contact sheet showing how one character language survives across multiple shots.

## Metadata Expectations

- Name the character, species, role, age band, silhouette traits, costume anchors, and recurring prop nouns.
- Record shot purpose, beat purpose, and whether the frame is for acting, continuity, blocking, timing, or editing.
- Capture toolchain context when relevant: GPT, Seedance 2.0, CapCut, previs, voice-directed prompting, or edit handoff.
- Keep labels short and repeated. Full canonical descriptions belong in prompt cards and research notes.

## Anti-Patterns

- Generic anime beauty shots with no production utility.
- Copyrighted franchise lookalikes or living-artist imitation.
- Moodboards that do not lock character proportions, props, or continuity.
- Mascot branding fluff without species, behavior, or shot logic.
- Dense unreadable text blocks that do not survive thumbnail or page view.
