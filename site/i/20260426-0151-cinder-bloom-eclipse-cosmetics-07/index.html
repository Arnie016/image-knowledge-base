Prompt Extract

Use case: environment-concept
Asset type: department-store window installation
Primary request: a street-facing window display for the Cinder Bloom eclipse cosmetics launch, with sculptural moon-disc plinths, oversized compact forms, suspended sample ribbons, layered product boxes, glowing half-circle mirrors, and one elegant mannequin head with dramatic makeup
Scene/background: urban evening sidewalk looking into a refined storefront, reflections on glass, passersby secondary
Subject: the window should feel like a temporary beauty installation designed to stop pedestrians, not a fashion apparel display
Style/medium: cinematic retail exterior photography
Composition/framing: straight-on storefront window view with clear depth from street reflections to display layers
Lighting/mood: blue-hour city light outside, warm copper interior glows, quietly theatrical
Color palette: black lacquer, pearl cream, apricot mineral tones, deep wine, moon-silver
Materials/textures: glass reflections, lacquer pedestals, foil paper, satin, brushed metal, polished stone
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no huge text decals, no holiday dressing, no clothing-store drift, must feel premium and beauty-specific
Avoid: Christmas window cliches, cyberpunk streets, generic luxury handbags, empty showroom minimalism
