Prompt Extract

Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: beauty product atlas contact sheet
Primary request: a dense catalog-style atlas of a fictional eclipse-themed cosmetics line called Cinder Bloom, showing powder compacts, refill pans, lip tints, perfume vials, applicator brushes, sampler cards, lacquered cases, and foil sample envelopes arranged as one coherent family
Scene/background: overhead studio table on warm stone with careful spacing, no hands
Subject: premium speculative beauty objects built from smoked glass, basalt-black resin, lunar silver hardware, copper foil seals, dusty apricot pigment, wine-red lacquer, and pearl cream paper
Style/medium: high-end catalog photography
Composition/framing: top-down contact sheet, many related objects, balanced clusters, clear negative space between families
Lighting/mood: soft museum-grade studio lighting, restrained highlights, luxurious but not flashy
Color palette: basalt black, dusty apricot, copper, moon-silver, cream, dried rose
Materials/textures: frosted glass, polished resin, powder pigment, brushed metal, embossed paper, silk ribbon
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no celebrity branding, no readable text required, must feel like a coherent commercial product system
Avoid: cyberpunk neon, generic perfume ads, sterile pharmacy packaging, glitter overload, plastic toy styling
