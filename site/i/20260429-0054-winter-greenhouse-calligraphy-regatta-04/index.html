Composition Notes

The paper hierarchy depends on folds, overlaps, drip marks, and one timing token instead of on big blocks of readable copy.

Prompt Extract

Use case: mixed-format
Asset type: handled paper suite for a fictional public regatta
Primary request: a top-down photographed suite of greenhouse regatta scorecards, mooring passes, wax-sealed lane assignments, folded canal map, stroke-order practice sheet, damp ticket ribbons, blotter cloth, pencil stub, and a brass timing token
Scene/background: arranged on wet cedar bench beside a greenhouse canal window with slight condensation drip marks, no hands
Style/medium: tactile paper-ephemera still life, municipal and handmade, not flat digital design
Composition/framing: square top-down frame with clear hierarchy, folds, overlaps, clipped corners, and enough breathing room to read as handled artifacts
Lighting/mood: cool diffuse winter daylight, quiet and studious
Color palette: cream paper, bottle green, faded burgundy, ink black, brass, damp cedar brown
Materials/textures: vellum, thick rag paper, wax seals, blotter fabric, water stains, softened corners, cedar grain, graphite smudges
Constraints: very little readable text and nothing critical; no logos; no watermark; must feel like real public event paperwork
Avoid: wedding invitation polish; luxury stationery mockup; generic scrapbook styling; flat vector poster look; fantasy calligraphy scrolls
Quality: high
