Composition Notes

The plate should feel dense but disciplined, with glass, paper, and seed forms alternating to keep the grid legible.

Prompt Extract

Use case: infographic-diagram
Asset type: scientific specimen plate for a speculative field-guide publishing branch
Primary request: a dense museum-grade specimen plate of nocturnal pollen collection tools and luminous meadow samples, including glass slides dusted with glowing pollen, seed heads, moth-wing reference cards, vellum envelopes, loupe hardware, tweezers, pin labels, wax-pencil circles, and tiny petri dishes
Scene/backdrop: dark indigo field-guide board photographed from directly above under careful reading-lamp light
Subject: thirty or more varied micro specimens and observation tools arranged with crisp scientific hierarchy and restrained label chips
Style/medium: documentary macro photography of a handled educational plate, tactile and precise rather than glossy
Composition/framing: full-frame top-down contact sheet with tight grid variation and deliberate breathing room
Lighting/mood: quiet midnight study light, soft hotspots, controlled shadow edges, faint phosphorescent glow in selected pollen traces
Color palette: indigo cloth, cream paper, tobacco vellum, moss green, pale amber, chalk white, tiny electric-cyan pollen glints
Materials/textures: paper fibers, glass reflections, seed fuzz, brushed brass, worn blackened steel, chalk dust
