Learning Value

Test whether a beauty-training world can stay documentary and socially legible instead of collapsing into generic wellness imagery.

Composition Notes

The foreground stations should establish the manicure practice logic while the atrium depth makes the school feel public and inhabited.

Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: documentary interior hero image
Primary request: a bustling but disciplined nail academy inside a tropical mall atrium where apprentices practice fossil-inspired manicures.
Scene/backdrop: three-story humid indoor mall atrium with glass roof, hanging ferns, terrazzo walkways, escalators in the distance, salon stations wrapping around pale stone display columns.
Subject: apprentice nail technicians in smoky mauve aprons practicing on seated models while instructors compare stone and shell reference samples.
Style/medium: high-end editorial documentary photography, realistic and richly detailed.
Composition/framing: wide 3/4 interior view from mezzanine height; active foreground stations, a teaching circle in the midground, skylit palms and mall circulation in the background.
Lighting/mood: filtered late-afternoon sunlight with humid glow and soft reflections.
Color palette: fossil gray terrazzo, shell pink, celadon green, amber resin, chrome silver, wet tropical greens.
Materials/textures: acrylic nails, polished stone, laminated training cards, chrome tools, tropical leaves, textured terrazzo.
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no dominant readable text, not a generic spa.
Avoid: beige luxury hotel minimalism, cyberpunk nightclub styling, cartoon proportions, direct imitation of any living artist.
