Prompt Extract

Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: commemorative souvenir family
Primary request: a family of tasteful souvenirs sold at the manual elevator cathedral: a round brass token tin, a pocket guide, stamped postcards, enamel floor badges, a boxed bell miniature, and a folded panorama card
Scene/background: arranged on pale limestone with one open tin and one opened presentation box, photographed like a museum shop catalog rather than luxury branding
Subject: coherent family of six to eight objects with clear hierarchy and believable public-institution design
Style/medium: editorial product photography, tactile, restrained, slightly worn edges
Composition/framing: square top-down layout with good spacing and one or two overlaps for depth
Lighting/mood: clear soft daylight, orderly, civic souvenir charm without cuteness
Color palette: cream, smoke blue, oxblood, tarnished brass, black ink
Materials/textures: enamel, stamped brass, linen paper, board box, ribbon, thin card stock
Text (verbatim): "MERIDIAN ASCENT"
Constraints: short text only, no logo clutter, no luxury perfume styling, no fantasy relic presentation, no watermark
Avoid: glossy gift-shop plastic, toy merchandising, modern minimalist startup branding, overpacked flat lay chaos
