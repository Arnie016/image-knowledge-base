Prompt Extract

Use case: speculative-object
Asset type: image knowledge base final PNG
Primary request: an orthographic family sheet of portable window-repair carts and folding support rigs designed for an apartment courtyard stained-glass picnic
Scene/background: neutral matte courtyard wall and pale concrete floor with soft shadow, presented like practical workshop equipment photography
Subject: three folding padded sawhorses, one slim wheeled glass-cart with blanket rolls, one narrow stair-climbing carrying frame, clamp-on edge guards, removable tool caddies, suction-cup storage hooks, and linen covers, all as a coherent buildable family
Style/medium: practical object-family photograph, halfway between workshop prototype sheet and municipal equipment catalog, not sci-fi industrial design
Composition/framing: frontal orthographic arrangement with each object fully visible and evenly spaced, slight overlap only for accessories
Lighting/mood: soft overcast daylight, plain and descriptive
Color palette: putty white, faded blue-gray, bottle green accents, rust brown, bare aluminum, worn canvas
Materials/textures: galvanized tube, chipped paint, scuffed wheels, stitched pads, dusty linen covers, taped corners
Constraints: no logos, no branding, no impossible mechanisms, no text labels, keep everything buildable from ordinary hardware-store parts
Avoid: futuristic robotics, glossy product renders
