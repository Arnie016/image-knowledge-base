Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: image knowledge base final PNG
Primary request: midnight storefront exterior of a velvet aquarium lost-and-found boutique on a quiet wet street
Scene/backdrop: narrow city street after rain, old masonry storefront, aquarium tanks glowing behind the glass, faint reflections on pavement
Subject: the boutique window showing trays of recovered objects, velvet curtains, brass lamps, small hand-lettered claim placards too small to read, and a lone customer pausing outside with a damp coat
Style/medium: documentary night photography, realistic and atmospheric
Composition/framing: eye-level wide shot of the full storefront with some sidewalk and upper facade visible, strong window reflections, no dramatic tilt
Lighting/mood: moody blue-green aquarium light balanced with amber interior lamps, tender nocturnal atmosphere
Color palette: teal, smoked navy, wet stone gray, brass amber, cream, faint coral notes
Materials/textures: rain-dark brick, fogged glass, velvet curtains, tarnished brass, wet pavement, aquarium glow
Constraints: no readable shop name, no logos, no fantasy creatures, no crowds, no cars dominating the frame
Avoid: cyberpunk neon overload, luxury boutique glamour, theme-park aquarium spectacle, fish-eye lens distortion
