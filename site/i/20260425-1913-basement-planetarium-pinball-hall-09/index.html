Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: silent comic page
Primary request: six-panel comic page about a basement planetarium pinball hall, following one local player from descending the stairs with a roll of score slips, through a repair bench handoff and a tense final ball, to walking back up into the quiet street after closing
Scene/background: same fictional hall with stairwell entry, machine rows, star dome, chalk leaderboard, and repair corner recurring across panels
Subject: one ordinary neighborhood player and the hall's mechanical world, keeping props and machines consistent across the sequence
Style/medium: richly inked comic illustration with muted painted color, tactile linework, and believable mechanical detail
Composition/framing: vertical six-panel page with clear action beats and one wider payoff panel near the end, no speech balloons
Lighting/mood: warm basement light, pale cyan projection speckles, modest nighttime melancholy at the end
Color palette: midnight blue, cream, amber, tobacco brown, chrome gray, muted cherry red
Materials/textures: paper score slips, scratched cabinets, chalkboard dust, worn stair rail, velvet curtain, chrome balls, inked shadows
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no superhero poses, no exaggerated manga effects, no text required for comprehension
Avoid: cyberpunk neon, cartoon slapstick, fantasy portals, glossy digital painting,
