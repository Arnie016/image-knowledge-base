Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: silent comic page
Primary request: a four-panel silent comic page about a young adult apprentice riding the original fictional Night Apiary Sleeper Train toward the first thawing blossom valley
Scene/background: panel 1 inside a dark berth hearing the warm hive cabinet at night; panel 2 frost window with first pale dawn and mountains; panel 3 platform pause with bundled workers and one paper pollen strip; panel 4 arrival into a luminous valley of early bloom seen from the rear observation deck
Subject: same apprentice in simple midnight coat and cream scarf throughout, no speech bubbles, no captions, continuity of clothing and train details
Style/medium: restrained painted comic page with natural textures, muted inks, cinematic realism rather than cartoon exaggeration
Composition/framing: one vertical page with four stacked panels, clear progression from close interior to open landscape, calm readable silhouettes
Lighting/mood: from amber night to blue dawn to pale blossom morning, reflective and hopeful without sentimentality
Color palette: midnight blue, cream, amber, frost gray, early blossom blush, pine black
Materials/textures: ink wash, paper grain, wool fabric, train glass, snow haze, timber railings
Constraints: no text; no logos; original world only; adult character; no manga style; no watermark
Avoid: superhero action; cute bee
