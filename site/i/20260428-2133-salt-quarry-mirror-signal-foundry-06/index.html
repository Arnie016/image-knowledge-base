Prompt Extract

Use case: material-study
Asset type: close-range workbench material study
Primary request: a quiet bench after mirror backing and salt-proofing work in a quarry signal foundry
Scene/background: handled timber bench in deep shade with chalk dust and fine salt crystals, cropped closely
Subject: a half-silvered mirror plate, blistered backing foil, leather visor, resin brush, angle gauge, cracked gloves, black shade cloth, brass fasteners, cloth wipe, and small tins of backing compound, all worn from real use
Style/medium: tactile documentary still life, not catalog photography
Composition/framing: close top-down three-quarter angle, dense but calm composition, emphasis on surface wear and residue
Lighting/mood: cool shade with one slash of reflected white quarry light, quiet and exhausted
Color palette: mirror silver, salt white, charcoal black, leather brown, dull brass, deep indigo cloth
Materials/textures: scratched glass, salt bloom, oily fingerprints, flaking foil, cracked leather, resin streaks, chalk dust, rough timber grain
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no pristine product styling, no decorative flowers or props
Avoid: luxury tabletop ad; glossy macro fetishism; medical sterile bench; colorful craft supplies
