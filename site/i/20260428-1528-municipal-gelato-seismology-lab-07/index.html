Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: civic event poster
Primary request: a printed poster for the fictional Municipal Gelato Seismology Lab Summer Readings Festival, mixing one architectural facade, layered gelato strata, tremor lines, spoons, bells, and small public crowds into a bold civic composition
Scene/background: poster shown as a single flat print artifact on a neutral wall, slightly worn paper edges
Subject: strong central building silhouette with radiating graph lines and stacked frozen color bands, designed as a real poster rather than a digital mockup
Style/medium: risograph-meets-screenprint poster design with tactile ink overlap and restrained illustration detail
Composition/framing: vertical poster with a loud title area at top, central emblematic scene, supporting icons and bands below, short decorative text allowed but not critical
Lighting/mood: sunlit photographed print, upbeat municipal summer energy
Color palette: cream paper, deep green, apricot orange, cherry red, charcoal black, muted steel gray
Materials/textures: paper grain, ink misregistration, pasted-wall wear, matte print finish
Constraints: must feel civic, playful, and legible at a glance; no logos; no watermark; no dense paragraphs; any text should be short and decorative
Avoid: corporate festival branding, slick ad polish, comic-con poster energy, neon gradients, generic ice cream parlor g
