Composition Notes

Prompt Extract

Use case: environment-concept
Asset type: architectural section illustration
Primary request: a cutaway section of a two-level fossil nail academy embedded in a tropical mall atrium, showing beauty training, retail display, and back-of-house prep in one coherent world.
Scene/backdrop: a vertical slice through the mall atrium with skylight roof, plant bridges, escalators, and rainwater channels feeding the greenery.
Subject: check-in desk, practice floor, shade library, micro-fossil prep lab, small classroom, wash station, stock wall, and mezzanine lounge.
Style/medium: architectural section rendering blended with tactile model-photography realism; precise but lush.
Composition/framing: wide section cut with clear room hierarchy and many small believable details; people included for scale.
Lighting/mood: bright skylight from above with deeper humid shadows below.
Color palette: pale stone, shell pink, celadon, amber, chrome, wet greens, fossil gray.
Materials/textures: terrazzo, glass, acrylic, chrome rails, paper samples, tropical foliage, resin displays.
Constraints: no giant text labels, no logos, no watermark, keep it plausible and buildable.
Avoid: sci-fi megastructure vibes, sterile blueprint flatness, generic shopping mall emptiness, direct imitation of any living artist.
