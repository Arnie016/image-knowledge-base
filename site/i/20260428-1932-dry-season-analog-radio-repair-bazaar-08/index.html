Composition Notes

The shelf rhythm and one offset keeper had to carry the image instead of any dramatic action.

Prompt Extract

Use case: environment-concept
Asset type: interior parts-room documentary
Primary request: a quiet spare-parts room inside an analog radio repair bazaar just before closing time
Scene/background: narrow upstairs room with shelves, shallow drawers, hanging coils, fan belts, old radio faces, wire spools, and a barred window catching the last light
Subject: one meticulous parts keeper sorting knobs and capacitors into labeled drawers, stacks of opened radio shells, a workstool, cloth bins, clipped guarantee slips, and a small tea glass near the ledger
Style/medium: documentary interior photography, intimate and unsentimental
Composition/framing: medium-wide room view with strong shelf rhythm, the keeper offset to one side, foreground bins leading inward
Lighting/mood: amber-blue closing light with soft fan-shadow patterns, patient and inventory-minded
Color palette: dusty teal, parchment cream, burnt umber wood, oxidized copper, smoke gray, amber glass
Materials/textures: worn drawer faces, hand-cut labels, dusty wire lacquer, chipped enamel bins, glass tea tumbler, cloth sacks
Constraints: no logos; no watermark; no modern plastic storage systems; keep it compact, overused, and believable
Avoid: cozy bookstore vibe; antique-shop nostalgia glamour; spotless workshop; excessive decorative clutter; sci-fi stockroom
