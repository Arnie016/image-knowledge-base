Prompt Extract

Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: commemorative recording and keepsake set for a speculative traveling moon opera
Primary request: a photographed product family consisting of a shellac record sleeve, folded libretto, stamped ticket envelope, small moon mask, lantern token, and rope-tied keepsake box from the moon-opera fairground
Scene/backdrop: tabletop product arrangement on worn dark wood with subtle paper backdrop, like a museum-shop catalog shoot for handled artifacts
Subject: the family should feel affordable, tactile, and public-event oriented rather than luxury merchandising
Style/medium: catalog product photography with realistic wear and restrained styling
Composition/framing: top-down and slight three-quarter mix in one arranged composition, with the record sleeve as the anchor and smaller keepsakes orbiting around it
Lighting/mood: soft studio light with gentle warmth, archival and intimate
Color palette: apricot, bone, indigo, oxblood, lamp black, dusty brass
Materials/textures: paper sleeves, shellac disc edge, stamped ticket stock, waxed cord, painted papier-mache, matte cardboard, brass token
Text (verbatim): none
Constraints: no logos; no watermark; no luxury jewelry-box styling; keep everything grounded in mass-public event ephemera
Avoid: polished vinyl lifestyle marketing; modern band merch; sci-fi collector's edition packaging; ornate fantasy relics
