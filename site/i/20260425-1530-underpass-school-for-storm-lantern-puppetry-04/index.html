Test whether the branch can support a believable public rehearsal scene at street scale without drifting into music-video fantasy.

Composition Notes

The foreground puddles and lantern reflections were essential because they turned the underpass into a stage without needing theatrical architecture.

Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: documentary performance still
Primary request: dusk rehearsal in a fictional underpass school for storm-lantern puppetry, with apprentices testing large illuminated puppets in a puddled service lane while one instructor watches timing marks and rope paths
Scene/background: broad city underpass with concrete columns, drainage channels, indigo tarps, scaffold poles, bicycles, utility carts, hanging ropes, and distant headlights softened by rain mist
Subject: 5 to 7 people actively rehearsing with illuminated fish, bird, moth, and cloud puppets, all clearly handmade from bamboo, waxed paper, patched silk, and copper wire
Style/medium: cinematic documentary photography, realistic public-space atmosphere
Composition/framing: wide establishing frame at eye level, foreground puddles reflecting lantern light, figures mid-action rather than posed, architecture legible but not monumental
Lighting/mood: after-rain blue hour with sodium-vapor amber practicals and soft city spill
Color palette: wet concrete gray, indigo tarp, lantern amber, oxidized copper, chalk white, muted safety orange
Materials/textures: rainwater reflections, chalk arrows on pavement, patched fabric, slick tarps, rough concrete, bamboo poles, mist
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no spectacle crowd, no fantasy magic, must feel like a community rehearsal under real infra
