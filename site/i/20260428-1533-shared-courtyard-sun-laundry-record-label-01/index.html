Prompt Extract

Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: hanging record-sleeve family portrait
Primary request: a dense but orderly family portrait of a tiny independent record label called Sun Laundry, showing many 7-inch vinyl singles in hand-stamped paper sleeves clipped to cotton clotheslines across a shared apartment courtyard
Scene/background: bright open-air courtyard with pale stucco walls and terrazzo below, no people in frame, shallow glimpse of balconies and striped towels behind the product family
Subject: cream and faded-coral paper sleeves, cobalt and tomato-red stamp graphics, simple center holes revealing bits of black vinyl, wooden clothespins, plastic milk crates, hand-cut track stickers, small speaker cables, folded lyric inserts peeking out
Style/medium: editorial catalog photography with documentary realism
Composition/framing: frontal wide composition with two or three parallel clotheslines running across the image, many related sleeves grouped by color and stamp motif, enough negative space to keep the family readable
Lighting/mood: hot late-morning sun with crisp shadows and warm reflected light, cheerful and domestic rather than commercial luxury
Color palette: cream, pale mint, faded coral, tomato red, cobalt blue, sun-washed aluminum, black vinyl
Materials/textures: matte paper, cotton rope, weathered wood clothespins, soft vinyl sheen, chalky stucco, terrazzo flecks
