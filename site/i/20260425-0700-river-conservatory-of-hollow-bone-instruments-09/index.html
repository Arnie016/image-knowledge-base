Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: silent comic page
Primary request: a four-panel vertical comic page showing a young conservatory apprentice carrying a repaired hollow bone instrument from the workshop, crossing a reed-lined footbridge, joining the twilight rooftop rehearsal, and finally sitting among the ensemble as the first note begins
Scene/background: river-town details and instrument cases recur across all panels for continuity
Subject: a quiet narrative about care, preparation, and communal music
Style/medium: finely inked and softly colored graphic novel page, tactile paper texture, restrained expressive faces
Composition/framing: four stacked panels with varied camera distance, clear storytelling silhouettes, no speech bubbles
Lighting/mood: late afternoon into blue-hour, tender and observant
Color palette: pale bone, indigo, dusk blue, warm amber, muted reeds green
Materials/textures: paper grain, wash color, cloth folds, shell charms, worn wood, river stone
Constraints: no dialogue balloons, no watermark, no superhero framing, no fantasy magic effects
Avoid: manga speed lines, glossy YA fantasy cover style, overcrowded page, extreme action poses
