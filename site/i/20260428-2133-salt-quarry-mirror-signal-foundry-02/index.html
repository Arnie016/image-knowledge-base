Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: editorial lineup portrait for a fictional industrial workforce
Primary request: a straight-on lineup of six salt-quarry mirror-signal workers standing against a chalk-white wall after a noon shift
Scene/background: quarry changing wall with hanging visor hooks, mirror cases, and dust marks, nothing theatrical
Subject: six adults in distinct but related signal uniforms with bleached canvas aprons, smoked-glass visors, indigo neck cloths, sun-whitened gloves, brass belt tools, and salt-stiff boots, faces sunburned and tired, each holding a different relay instrument
Style/medium: documentary editorial photograph, real human texture, restrained and buildable
Composition/framing: full-body lineup, centered, slight spacing between figures, camera at chest height, wide enough to show boots and wall detail
Lighting/mood: hard reflected quarry daylight, dry air, disciplined and unsentimental
Color palette: chalk white, bleached canvas, smoked glass black, dull brass, faded indigo, salt gray
Materials/textures: salt crust on hems, dust in seams, scratched visor glass, cracked leather straps, sweat-darkened cloth
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no glamor fashion pose, no futuristic costume armor, no heroic victory framing
Avoid: runway editorial; steampunk cosplay; military parade; glossy postproduction; smiling stock-photo energy
