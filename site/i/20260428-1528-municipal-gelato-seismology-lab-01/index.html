Prompt Extract

Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: civic food-science atlas contact sheet
Primary request: a dense top-down atlas for a fictional Municipal Gelato Seismology Lab, showing layered gelato core samples, scoop molds, basalt-black waffle cones, stainless tasting spoons, graph-paper tasting tickets, chilled sensor probes, freezer drawer labels, syrup vials, and terrazzo sample trays arranged as one coherent public-lab family
Scene/background: overhead studio table on pale municipal terrazzo, no hands
Subject: speculative but buildable dessert-science instruments and frozen specimens with civic utility rather than luxury branding
Style/medium: high-end catalog photography with documentary restraint
Composition/framing: top-down contact sheet, many related objects, balanced clusters, disciplined spacing, clear negative space between families
Lighting/mood: bright noon museum lighting, crisp shadows, clean but not glossy
Color palette: pistachio green, apricot sorbet, vanilla cream, stainless steel, basalt black, cherry-red graph ink, faded safety orange
Materials/textures: frosted steel, terrazzo stone, sugar crystal frost, waffle texture, printed graph paper, chilled glass, brushed aluminum
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no celebrity branding, no readable text required, must feel like a coherent municipal research branch
Avoid: generic ice cream shop styling, luxury dessert ad
