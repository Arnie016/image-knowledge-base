Composition Notes

The image relied on clean silhouette separation and grounded footwear so the figures felt tied to wet infrastructure rather than to a stage.

Prompt Extract

Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: character lineup
Primary request: full-body lineup of six apprentice storm-lantern puppeteers from a fictional underpass school, each carrying or wearing a different rain-performance rig built from bamboo poles, waxed paper lantern modules, patched silk sleeves, chalk-marked boots, and improvised safety gear
Scene/background: damp rehearsal lane under a city overpass with dark concrete columns, puddles, indigo tarps, and hanging ropes kept secondary
Subject: six workers and performers standing in a disciplined lineup, practical rather than glamorous, each silhouette clearly different and tied to one puppet role such as heron, carp, fox, moth, thunder drum, or bridge kite
Style/medium: editorial costume documentation with cinematic realism
Composition/framing: straight-on full-body lineup, equal spacing, readable silhouettes, minimal empty space, no fashion runway posing
Lighting/mood: after-rain dusk with warm work lights and cool ambient city light
Color palette: wet charcoal, indigo, lantern amber, oxidized copper, patched cream paper, small safety-orange accents
Materials/textures: rain-dark canvas, rubber boots, bamboo, copper wire, damp silk patches, waxed paper, chalk residue
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no superhero styling, no beauty retouching, must feel like municipal art apprentices and workers rather than fantasy heroes
