Prompt Extract

Use case: typographic-layout
Asset type: printed ephemera suite
Primary request: a photographed spread of printed artifacts from a speculative fossil nail academy: certificate booklet, appointment cards, foil sample sleeves, shade chips, folded receipts, training stamps, and wax-sealed care envelopes.
Scene/backdrop: top-down arrangement on a pale stone desk with a few chrome tools, shell fragments, and translucent resin samples.
Subject: coordinated paper and packaging pieces that feel handled, useful, and slightly ceremonial within a beauty-training context.
Style/medium: editorial product photography, tactile print-world realism.
Composition/framing: carefully composed flat lay with overlaps, pockets, tabs, and swatch groupings; short ornamental text only, not critical to understanding.
Lighting/mood: soft angled daylight with crisp paper shadows.
Color palette: cream paper, shell pink, celadon, amber, oxidized silver, fossil gray.
Materials/textures: coated cards, matte paper, foil, wax seals, acetate sleeves, rubber stamps.
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no dense readable paragraphs, keep the typography decorative and secondary.
Avoid: wedding invitation luxury, generic cosmetics branding, office-stationery plainness, direct imitation of any living artist.
