Prompt Extract

Use case: environment-concept
Asset type: improvised weather-shift public listening booth
Primary request: a brief summer rain has transformed the Sun Laundry courtyard into an improvised listening booth under blue tarps and clotheslines, with residents sheltering around portable speakers and turntables while puddles gather on terrazzo
Scene/background: same shared apartment courtyard, now partially covered with blue plastic tarps clipped to balcony rails and laundry lines, paper sleeves lifted away from the rain, warm windows beginning to glow
Subject: folding chairs, milk crates, sheltered turntable table, bundled extension cords, clipped sleeves drying high under the tarp, a few neighbors standing close and listening
Style/medium: cinematic documentary photography
Composition/framing: wide corner view with foreground puddles and tarp edges, midground listening cluster under the shelter, background balconies and wet stucco walls
Lighting/mood: blue-grey rain light mixed with warm interior window glow, intimate and improvised rather than dramatic
Color palette: blue tarp, cream paper, cobalt ink, faded coral, wet charcoal terrazzo, warm amber windows
Materials/textures: wet plastic tarp, rain-dark stucco, matte paper, vinyl sheen, puddle reflections, cotton rope, aluminum chair legs
Constraints: no storm-apocalypse mood, no emergency-response imagery, no readable text dependen
