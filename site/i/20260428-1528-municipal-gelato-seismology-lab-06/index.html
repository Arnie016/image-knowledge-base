Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: street documentary still
Primary request: a field reading shift in a bright civic plaza where two technicians from a fictional Municipal Gelato Seismology Lab compare spoon-vibration readings beside a small survey cart while residents sample tiny cups and pass by
Scene/background: Mediterranean-style city square with stone paving, shade umbrellas, tram tracks, municipal buildings, and midday pedestrian life
Subject: one technician holding a tuning spoon to a metal railing, another marking a graph card on a clipboard, children with sample cups nearby, the cart stocked with cones, stainless jars, and analog recording hardware
Style/medium: candid documentary photo
Composition/framing: medium-wide street photograph, slight diagonal motion through the square, layered foreground and background activity, no posed subjects
Lighting/mood: hard summer daylight with pockets of shade, busy but calm public-service atmosphere
Color palette: sun-bleached cream stone, pistachio and apricot accents, faded orange umbrellas, dark railings, berry-red graph lines
Materials/textures: stone pavement, chipped paint, steel fittings, paper cards, melting gelato edges, canvas shade cloth
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no commercial campaign look, must feel like a public research ritual integrated into everyday city life
Avoid: stock-photo smiles, touris
