Prompt Extract

Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: knowledge-base visual research final PNG
Primary request: a photographed poster-and-ticket suite for a fictional rooftop meteor choir winter performance.
Scene/backdrop: worn concrete wall and shallow shelf ledge in a rehearsal stairwell.
Subject: one large screenprinted event poster, several narrow ticket strips, an admission card, seal stickers, and a folded handbill, all from the same performance identity system.
Style/medium: tactile print ephemera photographed as real handled objects, serious and atmospheric.
Composition/framing: the poster dominates vertically while tickets and cards rest along the ledge below, with a few pinned corners and soft curling paper.
Lighting/mood: low stairwell light with a warm wall lamp and cool ambient spill, restrained and ceremonial.
Color palette: charcoal, deep teal, cream, pale apricot, brass pinheads, weathered black ink.
Materials/textures: screenprint grain, paper tooth, tape residue, curled ticket edges, pinholes, concrete dust.
Text (verbatim): visible text should stay brief and non-critical; rely on symbols, dates, and short titles more than paragraphs.
Constraints: fictional event only, no logos, no watermark, no celebrity concert branding.
Avoid: glossy arena-poster polish, rainbow gradients, dense readable copy, pop-star glamour, festival sponsor walls.
