Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: beauty retail interior campaign still
Primary request: a blue-hour interior view of a speculative cosmetics launch called Cinder Bloom inside a refined department-store beauty hall, with a curved test counter, glowing mirror niches, eclipse-shaped product pedestals, makeup artists, browsing customers, trays of compacts, perfume samplers, and one tall sculptural lightbox
Scene/background: elegant retail hall with dark stone floor, smoked mirrors, pale plaster, and a softly lit ceiling
Subject: the retail environment itself should sell a premium eclipse-mineral cosmetics collection without relying on readable branding
Style/medium: editorial retail photography
Composition/framing: wide but intimate three-quarter interior view, counter dominant, people secondary, clear product zones
Lighting/mood: blue-hour ambient light outside, warm interior glow, cinematic but believable
Color palette: basalt black, moon-silver, dusty apricot, copper, cream, deep wine
Materials/textures: smoked glass, polished stone, lacquered trays, brushed metal, velvet stools, paper sample cards
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no giant readable text, no sterile airport or mall vibe, must feel like a high-end temporary launch installation
Avoid: cyberpunk neon, generic luxury boutique, fashion runway, empty showroom, purple lighting
