Use case: infographic-diagram

Composition/framing: vertical poster filling most of the frame, clear route lines and icon clusters, generous margins, visible paper texture, slightly off-register ink for realism
Lighting/mood: bright indirect daylight with mild wall shadow, practical and charming
Color palette: cream paper, cobalt ink, faded coral ink, touches of tomato red tape, pale mint wall
Materials/textures: fibrous paper, soy ink, tape, pinholes, chalk dust, stucco grain
Constraints: minimal readable text, map should remain understandable through icons and paths, no brand logos beyond the invented title, no watermark
