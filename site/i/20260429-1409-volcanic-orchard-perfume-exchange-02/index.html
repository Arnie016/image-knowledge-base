Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: editorial documentary photograph for a speculative public fragrance exchange
Primary request: the main hall of a volcanic orchard perfume exchange at blue hour, where growers, samplers, brokers, and buyers inspect chilled scent lots beneath a glazed caldera roof
Scene/backdrop: a multi-level public exchange hall carved into dark volcanic stone, with open fruit-crate counters, bottle-testing stations, cooling vents, and a glimpse of orchard terraces outside through rain-specked glass
Subject: people in weathered aprons and ash-resistant coats comparing blotter tiles, carrying shallow cooled crates, examining bottle flights, and marking compact ledgers
Style/medium: documentary interior photography, believable and human, not fantasy concept art
Composition/framing: wide eye-level interior shot with layered foreground counters, active midground figures, and a high roof structure receding into the back; one strong aisle line leading through the exchange
Lighting/mood: blue-hour skylight mixed with amber task lamps and cool condensation glow, busy but hushed atmosphere
Color palette: basalt charcoal, leaf green, amber glass, sulfur cream paper, copper, bruised plum, wet silver
Materials/textures: wet stone floor, frosted glass bottles, wax seals, crate wood, rain-specked glazing, wool, canvas, brushed metal, fruit skins
Constraints: origi
