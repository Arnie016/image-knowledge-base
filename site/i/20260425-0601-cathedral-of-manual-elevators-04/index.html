Composition Notes

The vertical hierarchy from crypt to tower must stay legible at a glance.

Prompt Extract

Use case: mixed-format
Asset type: monumental architectural section cutaway
Primary request: a vast cross-section cutaway of a limestone cathedral organized around multiple manual elevator shafts, showing crypt level, nave, gallery, clerical bridge, bell tower, attendant stations, waiting chapels, rope-and-counterweight rooms, and daylight pouring through the upper structure
Scene/background: the entire building shown as one educational section plate, with the surrounding district hinted at lightly below the foundation
Subject: architecture is the star; tiny human figures provide scale but remain secondary
Style/medium: architectural illustration blended with museum exhibit plate, restrained and believable
Composition/framing: wide section across the full frame, clear vertical hierarchy from basement to tower top, no dramatic fisheye distortion
Lighting/mood: crisp afternoon light entering from one side, calm and public rather than apocalyptic
Color palette: limestone, soot gray, muted oxblood, smoke blue, brass accents
Materials/textures: cut stone, iron gates, timber platforms, pulley cables, worn stair treads, glass clerestory windows
Constraints: minimal non-critical labeling only, no fantasy flying buttress exaggeration, no steampunk machinery overload, no logo, no watermark
Avoid: video-game dungeon styling, explosive destruction, cathedral ruin romanticism, blueprint fla
