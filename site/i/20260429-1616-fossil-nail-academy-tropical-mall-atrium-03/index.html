Prompt Extract

Use case: atlas-contact-sheet
Asset type: retail product and tool study
Primary request: a dense contact-sheet atlas of mineral polishes, amber-resin practice fingers, fossil-texture buffers, stone sample swatches, chrome manicure tools, foil wraps, and shell-inspired decals from a speculative fossil nail academy.
Scene/backdrop: top-down pale stone worktable with careful grid spacing and a few pinned sample notes rendered as abstract marks rather than readable text.
Subject: 24 to 30 related objects arranged by function and color family, each believable as salon training equipment or retail product.
Style/medium: catalog photography, orthographic and highly tactile.
Composition/framing: top-down full-frame atlas, dense but legible grouping, contact-sheet discipline.
Lighting/mood: soft museum-table lighting with crisp edges and gentle shadows.
Color palette: amber, shell pink, celadon, chalk cream, smoky quartz, chrome, fossil gray.
Materials/textures: glossy gel bottles, matte stone swatches, translucent practice tips, brushed metal tools, foil packets, resin trays.
Constraints: no brand logos, no watermark, minimal unreadable annotation only.
Avoid: medical instrument coldness, generic cosmetics flat lay, rainbow clutter, direct imitation of any living artist.
