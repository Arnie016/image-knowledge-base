Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: exterior environment photograph for a speculative hospitality site
Primary request: a moonlit exterior view of a cliffside mineral bathhouse with lantern stairways, open-air terraces, glowing steam pools, robe-clad guests, and waves breaking below, seen as a living place rather than a brochure rendering
Scene/background: night coastal cliff with the bathhouse embedded in stone, sea horizon visible, no surrounding city skyline
Subject: the bathhouse architecture, terraces, lanterns, and water surfaces, with people small and secondary
Style/medium: documentary-like twilight architecture photography with tactile realism, calm long-exposure atmosphere
Composition/framing: wide three-quarter exterior view from a nearby overlook, layered terraces descending diagonally, good depth from foreground stone to sea beyond
Lighting/mood: blue-hour moonlight mixed with warm lantern glow and soft steam illumination, ceremonial and hushed
Color palette: deep navy sky, warm amber lanterns, limestone gray, teal water, indigo robes, pale steam
Materials/textures: wet stone, lime plaster, cedar slats, pool water reflections, brass lantern cages, cliff vegetation
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no giant resort tower, no neon, no fireworks, no living-artist imitation
Avoid: postcard cliches, Mediterranean hotel marketing, fantasy fortress look, crowde
