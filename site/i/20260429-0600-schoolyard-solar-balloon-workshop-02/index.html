Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: image knowledge base final PNG
Primary request: a dawn documentary scene of students inflating a handmade solar balloon on a public-school blacktop before classes
Scene/backdrop: fenced schoolyard with painted court lines, low classroom buildings, chain-link shadows, rooftop vents, and a pale sunrise sky
Subject: two teachers and several middle-school students carefully spreading a patched black-and-orange balloon envelope across the blacktop while portable fans fill it, one student carries stitched sandbag weights, another checks a chalked launch grid, the balloon beginning to billow upward
Style/medium: grounded documentary photography, candid and real, no fantasy spectacle
Composition/framing: wide eye-level scene with the balloon envelope dominating foreground and midground, people arranged naturally, clear depth to the school buildings beyond
Lighting/mood: crisp dawn sunlight with long cool shadows, anticipatory and communal
Color palette: charcoal blacktop, sky blue, citrus orange, cream fabric, silver patch tape, faded gym-red paint
Materials/textures: wrinkled ripstop nylon, chalk lines, chain-link fence sparkle, worn sneakers, utilitarian school coats, plastic fan housings
Constraints: keep it present-day and buildable, no logos, no readable signage, no cinematic explosions or festival fireworks
Avoid: inspirational ad poli
