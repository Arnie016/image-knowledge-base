Composition Notes

Prompt Extract

Use case: mixed-format
Asset type: ceremonial fashion lineup and public-attendant character sheet
Primary request: a formal lineup of six people from the manual elevator cathedral world: three elevator attendants and three pilgrims, each wearing distinct but related garments for different floors and social roles
Scene/background: plain limestone wall with a narrow stone ledge, no extra furniture, no crowd
Subject: full-body front-facing lineup with subtle turn variations, showing long coats, gloves, queue badges, numbered sashes, velvet capes, brass key rings, and passport satchels
Style/medium: editorial costume lookbook photographed like a museum fashion plate, realistic fabric detail
Composition/framing: wide vertical lineup, equal spacing, each figure fully visible from head to shoe, generous breathing room
Lighting/mood: cool noon daylight, disciplined and civic rather than theatrical
Color palette: ash cream, smoke blue, oxblood, soot black, oxidized brass
Materials/textures: wool, velvet trim, brushed brass buttons, stamped linen badges, polished leather shoes
Constraints: no halos, no fantasy armor, no circus styling, no modern casualwear, no logos, no watermark
Avoid: masquerade-opera excess, steampunk goggles, runway drama, celebrity portrait posing
