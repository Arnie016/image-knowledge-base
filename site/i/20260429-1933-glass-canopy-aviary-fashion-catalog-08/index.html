Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: silent comic page for a speculative aviary world
Primary request: a six-panel wordless comic page about a child returning a found feather token through the glass-canopy aviary and being guided to the archive counter before looking up into the birds overhead
Scene/backdrop: the same conservatory world across panels with entry hall, canopy walk, quiet archive desk, mist court, and final upward view under glass
Subject: one child visitor, one calm steward in a weather cape, a found feather in a clear sleeve, and birds crossing through the page rhythm
Style/medium: elegant watercolor-and-ink comic page with clean panel borders and strong environmental continuity
Composition/framing: six varied panels arranged vertically and horizontally with readable motion and one large final panel
Lighting/mood: soft daytime light, tender and observant rather than sentimental
Color palette: celadon, apricot, fog white, moss green, graphite, pale teal
Materials/textures: watercolor washes, pencil line, paper grain, translucent sleeve, wet tile reflections
Constraints: no speech balloons; no logos; no watermark; keep expressions subtle and actions clear
Avoid: manga exaggeration; superhero action; children's-book cuteness overload; generic zoo outing
