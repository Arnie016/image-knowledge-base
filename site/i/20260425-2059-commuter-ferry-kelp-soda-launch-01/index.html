Prompt Extract

Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: flavor-family atlas for a regional soft drink launch
Primary request: a dense catalog-style product photograph showing a fictional commuter-ferry kelp soda brand with eight distinct cans, three short glass bottles, loose caps, crate dividers, route-color neck tags, and a few sliced citrus garnishes arranged as one coherent flavor family
Scene/background: chilled brushed-aluminum tabletop in a ferry-terminal prep room, faint harbor daylight reflections, no people
Subject: sparkling kelp-and-citrus soda packaging that feels practical, local, and commuter-facing rather than luxury or novelty, with flavors differentiated by small route-color accents and simple wave-knot icon marks
Style/medium: premium catalog photography with documentary realism
Composition/framing: top-down three-quarter hybrid contact-sheet composition, dense but organized, every container fully visible, shallow negative space only at the edges
Lighting/mood: bright salt-air midday light, cool highlights, soft shadowing, restrained editorial finish
Color palette: sea-glass green, cream, citrus yellow, galvanized silver, faded navy, safety orange accents
Materials/textures: cold aluminum, painted cans, embossed caps, wet glass, paper neck tags, condensation, crate fiberboard, citrus peel
Constraints: no logos from real brands, no watermark, no mascot, no biotech futurism, no c
