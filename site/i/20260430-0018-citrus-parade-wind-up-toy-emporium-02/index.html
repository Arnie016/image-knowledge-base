Prompt Extract

Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: knowledge-base visual research final PNG
Primary request: a catalog-style lineup of six related wind-up parade toys from a fictional citrus-themed toy universe.
Scene/backdrop: clean cream seamless studio backdrop with faint floor shadow.
Subject: six full toys shown front-facing with slight three-quarter rotation variety: orange drum bear, lemon baton rabbit, lime crocodile horn player, grapefruit swan float, pomelo elephant balloon puller, kumquat scooter dog.
Style/medium: highly detailed product-design render photographed like a 1970s toy catalog, whimsical but physically plausible.
Composition/framing: straight-on wide composition with all six toys evenly spaced and fully visible, enough breathing room around each silhouette.
Lighting/mood: bright soft studio light, cheerful and crisp.
Color palette: saturated citrus colors with cobalt, cherry red, cream, and brushed tin accents.
Materials/textures: slightly scuffed plastic shells, painted tin wheels, cloth pennants, metal wind-up keys, decal details.
Constraints: fictional character family only, no brand logos, no watermark, keep proportions toy-like rather than mascot-costume-like.
Avoid: human faces, luxury product minimalism, dark background, muddy textures, realistic animals, dense readable text.
