Prompt Extract

Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: accessory and beauty kit family
Primary request: a refined product-family shot of desert opera wardrobe accessories arranged as a coherent collectible set, including scent vials, compact powder tins, brass combs, veil pins, ribbon spools, glove packets, moonstone-like costume buttons, and labeled makeup cases
Scene/background: controlled neutral studio setting with shallow stage-like risers and soft shadow
Subject: one accessory family from a traveling wardrobe exchange, tactile and transport-ready rather than luxurious
Style/medium: premium catalog photography with restraint and craft realism
Composition/framing: grouped family portrait of products, clear silhouettes, slight three-quarter camera angle, disciplined spacing
Lighting/mood: soft studio light with gentle highlights and warm undertone
Color palette: tarnished brass, dusty plum, smoked teal, cream, charcoal, sand
Materials/textures: frosted glass vials, stitched fabric wraps, engraved metal, worn paper labels, velvet-lined boxes, lacquered wood
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no modern cosmetic branding, keep it artisanal and coherent
Avoid: glossy beauty-ad clichés, celebrity perfume campaign look, plastic packaging, excessive sparkle, ornate fantasy relics
