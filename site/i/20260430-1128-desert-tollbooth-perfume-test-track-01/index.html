Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: final PNG for an image research knowledge base
Primary request: documentary roadside photo of a speculative desert toll plaza where each lane is also a perfume test station, with drivers pausing under sun-faded canopies while attendants in pale work suits hand out scent strips and stamp heat-curled receipts
Scene/backdrop: wide asphalt test track in a salt desert basin with bleached toll islands, shade canopies, low mountains, fuel drums converted into planters, and mirage shimmer in the distance
Subject: the ordinary public-service logic of fragrance testing merged with road travel, with two or three modest vehicles, pump-like scent dispensers, lane beacons, shade curtains, and practical attendants
Style/medium: photorealistic editorial photography, natural detail, restrained speculative realism
Composition/framing: wide eye-level establishing shot, strong horizontal lanes, one dominant canopy group, enough depth to show multiple testing lanes without becoming a crowd scene
Lighting/mood: brutal noon sunlight, hard shadows, dry heat, utilitarian but theatrical atmosphere
