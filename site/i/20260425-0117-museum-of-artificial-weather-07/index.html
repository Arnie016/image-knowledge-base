Prompt Extract

Use case: environment-concept
Asset type: museum installation interior
Primary request: a dramatic but believable interior view of the fictional Museum of Artificial Weather's lightning corridor, where visitors walk through a glass-lined chamber of controlled electrical arcs, glowing instruments, and reflective weather-metal details
Scene/background: long gallery corridor inside a civic museum, with the installation occupying most of the frame and a few visitors present for scale
Subject: Faraday-like glass chambers, branching white-blue arcs, brass railings, clouded glass panels, weather icons in relief, and quiet visitor silhouettes observing the phenomenon
Style/medium: cinematic architectural interior photography with realistic museum lighting and tactile materials, not sci-fi action art
Composition/framing: one-point perspective down the corridor, strong depth, clear path through the middle, installation elements contained to the sides and ceiling, no cluttered signage
Lighting/mood: dim gallery with cold electrical flashes and warm floor-level guide lighting, eerie but safe and public
Color palette: storm blue, charcoal, pale electric white, oxidized brass, gray terrazzo, smoked glass
Materials/textures: glass, polished metal, terrazzo, matte painted walls, fine condensation, faint haze, insulated cables hidden cleanly
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no cyberpunk cit
