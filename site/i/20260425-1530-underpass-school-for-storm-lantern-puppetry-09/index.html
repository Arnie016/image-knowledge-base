Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: comic page
Primary request: six-panel comic page about a fictional underpass storm-lantern puppetry school on parade night, showing final repairs, chalk marks on wet pavement, lantern ignition, a nervous apprentice lifting a fox rig, the procession entering floodwater, and the crowd seeing the glowing puppets reflected beneath the bridge
Scene/background: the same rain-dark city underpass world carried across all panels, with concrete columns, indigo tarps, ropes, bamboo poles, puddles, and modest public spectators
Subject: practical workers and apprentices moving handmade lantern puppets through a sequence of preparation and release, no speech balloons required
Style/medium: richly textured comic illustration with documentary realism and restrained graphic stylization
Composition/framing: vertical page with varied panel sizes, one wide payoff panel near the bottom, strong continuity of props and materials across panels
Lighting/mood: blue-hour into midnight, warm lantern light growing panel by panel
Color palette: charcoal, indigo, amber, oxidized copper, damp cream paper, chalk white, minimal safety orange accents
Materials/textures: wet concrete, waxed paper lantern skins, patched silk, bamboo ribs, chalk dust, puddle reflections
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no anime style imitation, no superhero action, no fantasy spell effec
