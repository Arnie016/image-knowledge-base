Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: documentary interior still
Primary request: wide interior photograph of a fictional basement planetarium pinball hall during early evening opening hour, with rows of celestial pinball machines under a small domed star projector, players standing at a few tables, a coin counter, velvet curtains, and hand-painted constellation side art
Scene/background: low-ceiling basement venue with tiled steps from the street, plaster walls, star charts, ticket corkboard, and a modest snack shelf
Subject: the hall itself as a neighborhood leisure space, with machines, stools, paper score slips, chrome rails, and a small planetarium dome glowing above the center row
Style/medium: photorealistic editorial interior photography
Composition/framing: wide eye-level frame from one corner, enough depth to read machine rows and dome structure, center kept mostly clear
Lighting/mood: warm incandescent bulbs mixed with dim celestial projection light, inviting rather than flashy
Color palette: midnight blue, cream, tarnished chrome, tobacco brown wood, amber bulbs, pale cyan star-map projection
Materials/textures: scuffed lino floor, painted steel cabinets, smoked acrylic, worn ticket paper, dusty velvet curtains, coin scratches
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no branded machines, no futuristic holograms, must feel local, mechanical, and slightly handmade
