Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: silent comic page
Primary request: a four-panel silent comic about an apprentice mirror-signal worker recalibrating a relay after a sand gust in a salt quarry
Scene/background: the same quarry tower and white terraces across all panels, with shade cloth, mirror frame, and distant relay stations kept consistent
Subject: one young adult apprentice in a bleached apron and smoked visor clearing dust, checking an angle card, re-aiming the mirror, then watching a successful flash answer from a distant tower
Style/medium: restrained comic illustration with dusty realism, clear panels, no speech bubbles
Composition/framing: four equal horizontal panels stacked vertically, each panel with a distinct action beat and readable silhouette
Lighting/mood: late afternoon dry light turning cooler, quiet competence rather than triumph
Color palette: salt white, faded indigo, brass, smoked black, leather brown, pale sky blue
Materials/textures: chalk dust, scratched glass, cloth folds, timber tower planks, quarry grit
Constraints: no text, no logos, no watermark, no exaggerated cartoon comedy, maintain costume continuity across panels
Avoid: manga speed lines; superhero posing; lush fantasy sky; glossy cel shading; overcrowded panel backgrounds
