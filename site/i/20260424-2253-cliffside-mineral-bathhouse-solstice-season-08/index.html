Composition Notes

Close three-quarter tabletop composition with one hero dish and clearly legible supporting vessels.

Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: tabletop food and tea service photograph for a fictional hospitality world
Primary request: a cooling dessert and herbal tea service from a cliffside mineral bathhouse, arranged on a low stone table with chilled poached pears, translucent herb jellies, citrus peel syrup, glazed ceramic cups, a brass tea pot, folded napkins, and small salt-cured sweets
Scene/background: intimate terrace tabletop near dusk or early night, no people visible, sea and lantern blur only in the far background
Subject: an original bathhouse refreshment service that feels tied to the same world as the robes, tokens, and stone architecture
Style/medium: restrained editorial food photography, tactile and believable, no commercial gloss overload
Composition/framing: close three-quarter tabletop view with one hero pear dish, supporting jellies and tea vessels clearly visible, balanced asymmetry
Lighting/mood: warm lantern edge light with cool ambient twilight fill, quiet restorative mood
Color palette: pear green, amber syrup, indigo napkin, limestone, brass gold, translucent herb tones
Materials/textures: glazed ceramic, hammered brass, wet stone, folded linen, syrup sheen, translucent jelly, citrus peel, tea steam
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no modern glassware set, no plated fine-dining gimmicks, no living-artist imitation
Avoid: restaurant ad styling
