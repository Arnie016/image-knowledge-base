Use case: stylized-concept

Subject: the recognizable internal code of a mountain monastery where bread ovens and ceremonial kites share one safety culture
Style/medium: high-detail studio-documentary tableau, symmetrical but handmade, tactile realism rather than luxury product render
Composition/framing: straight-on lineup with twelve to fifteen objects and garments in careful spacing, full silhouettes visible, minimal shadows, centered composition
Lighting/mood: soft cold daylight with gentle bounce, calm severe tone
Color palette: soot black, ash white, indigo cloth, oxidized copper, pale rice paper, ochre twine, small vermilion seal marks
Materials/textures: singed paper edges, flour dust, brushed copper, stitched canvas, braided hemp, rough plaster
Constraints: no brand labels, no modern plastics, no glossy ecommerce styling, no unreadable cluttered text
