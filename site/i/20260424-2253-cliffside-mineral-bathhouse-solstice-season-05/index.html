Prompt Extract

Use case: mixed-format
Asset type: architectural cutaway plate for a speculative hospitality building
Primary request: a dramatic but believable architectural section of a cliffside mineral bathhouse carved into coastal stone, revealing stacked soaking pools, cedar steam chambers, cooling galleries, robe lockers, tea terraces, lantern stairs, and wave-level salt rooms in one coherent cross-section
Scene/background: full-building section seen from the side with the sea and cliff visible, presented like a printed architectural plate rather than a concept-art matte painting
Subject: one original bathhouse complex with clear circulation and repeated material language, no unrelated town around it
Style/medium: meticulous architectural illustration with tactile realism, part section drawing and part model-photograph sensibility
Composition/framing: wide horizontal cutaway occupying most of the frame, readable stacked zones, no tiny labels needed, a few small human silhouettes for scale only if helpful
Lighting/mood: late golden-hour light outside, warm steam glow inside, calm ceremonial atmosphere
Color palette: limestone, warm cedar, muted teal water, indigo accents, brass lantern light, sea-gray rock
Materials/textures: chiseled stone, polished plaster, cedar slats, tiled pool edges, wet steps, brass rail details, soft steam
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no sci-fi machinery,
