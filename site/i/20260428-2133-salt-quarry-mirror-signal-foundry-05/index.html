Prompt Extract

Use case: environment-concept
Asset type: architectural cutaway and section plate
Primary request: a precise sectional view of a salt-cliff mirror-signal foundry built into a quarry wall
Scene/background: stepped white quarry cliff with visible interior rooms, lifts, platforms, shade courts, polishing benches, mirror storage, drafting room, and rooftop relay deck
Subject: one coherent communication-manufacturing complex showing how raw salt quarry infrastructure supports mirror casting, backing, calibration, paperwork, and relay dispatch
Style/medium: architectural section illustration with tactile realism, restrained labels optional but not necessary, no fantasy ornament
Composition/framing: wide cross-section with cliff face cut open, vertical circulation visible, clear room hierarchy, humans small but present for scale
Lighting/mood: bright exterior noon light and cool shaded interior pockets, procedural and intelligible
Color palette: salt white, chalk gray, bleached timber, dull brass, smoked glass black, faded indigo cloth accents
Materials/textures: crystalline quarry walls, plank walkways, dust, glass sheets, leather shades, paper stacks, pulley ropes
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no giant futuristic reactors, no lush decoration, no unreadable dense labels
Avoid: sci-fi megastructure; fantasy dwarven mine; infographic clutter; cozy cottage interiors
