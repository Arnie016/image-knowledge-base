Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: aftermath still life
Primary request: a quiet mask-drying and foil-repair bench after the municipal steam-bath fencing league finals
Scene/background: pine bench against cream tile and fogged glass, low shelf of eucalyptus soap tins, folded towels, damp floor reflections nearby
Subject: mesh masks hung to dry, foils laid open for repair, wrapped grips, brass locker tags, chalk score slips, a whistle, cotton jackets draped over the bench, one half-empty tea glass, signs of use but no mess for its own sake
Style/medium: photorealistic documentary still life, intimate and unsentimental
Composition/framing: medium-close horizontal frame, bench angled slightly, clear depth layers, focus on the handled objects and moisture
Lighting/mood: late afternoon steam-softened light, tired and calm after competition
Color palette: damp pine, cream tile, charcoal mesh, white cotton, brass, pale eucalyptus green, muted red traces
Materials/textures: wet wood sheen, terry cloth, metal mesh, rubber grips, paper slips, soap residue, condensation
Constraints: no logos; no watermark; no luxury spa product styling; keep the scene public, modest, and believable
Avoid: pristine product photography; dramatic cinematic darkness; clutter chaos; ornate props; staged bouquet aesthetics
