Composition Notes

Prompt Extract

Use case: mixed-format
Asset type: material and pigment board
Primary request: a tactile beauty-material study board for the Cinder Bloom launch, showing pigment smears, pressed powder discs, lacquer chips, foil samples, ribbon swatches, paper stocks, metal hardware, and small component fragments arranged like a designer's review board
Scene/background: overhead composition on neutral cream paper with pin shadows and careful spacing
Subject: the board should explain the material language of an eclipse-themed cosmetics collection without becoming a technical engineering sheet
Style/medium: editorial materials-board photography
Composition/framing: top-down board with labeled zones implied by grouping, not by heavy text
Lighting/mood: soft daylight studio light, crisp but gentle, calm and tactile
Color palette: dusty apricot, deep wine, black lacquer, cream, silver, muted copper
Materials/textures: powder pigment, foil paper, satin ribbon, glossy resin, brushed aluminum, vellum, embossed board
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, minimal or no readable text, must feel like a creative director's tactile review surface
Avoid: industrial manufacturing blueprint, office flat lay, scrapbook clutter, school art-board vibe, generic craft supplies
