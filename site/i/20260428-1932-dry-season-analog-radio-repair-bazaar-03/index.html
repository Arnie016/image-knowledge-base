Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: street documentary still
Primary request: a wide view down a dry-season analog radio repair bazaar lane at peak noon business
Scene/background: densely packed market alley with patched awnings, hanging telescoping antennas, shelves of opened transistor sets, fan cages, chalk repair boards, shallow dust, and distant rooftops glowing in heat
Subject: several repair stalls in active use, customers waiting on stools, one bench with open chassis and solder smoke, runners carrying boxed radios, stacked parts drawers, and cloth-wrapped repaired sets ready for pickup
Style/medium: documentary street photography with tactile realism, no travel-brochure polish
Composition/framing: slightly elevated wide frame looking down the lane, foreground stall detail, midground labor, layered background depth
Lighting/mood: brutal dry noon light broken into stripes by awnings, severe but alive
Color palette: faded teal paint, chalk beige masonry, burnt rust dust, amber dial glass, tarnished metal, muted maroon cloth
Materials/textures: chipped plaster, canvas tarps, Bakelite, metal fan cages, corrugated shutters, paper tags, dust bloom
Constraints: no logos; no watermark; no digital billboards; no luxury storefronts; keep it practical, crowded, and local
Avoid: cyberpunk market clichés; tourist-postcard styling; oversaturated color; polished mall retail;
