Composition Notes

The room needed readable architecture and red score bulbs around the central action so the sport never floated free of the bathhouse.

Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: documentary sports scene
Primary request: a live municipal steam-bath fencing league bout inside a skylit public bathhouse at noon
Scene/background: long wet-tile piste set between old plunge pools and pine spectator benches, fogged clerestory windows, red score bulbs, damp towels hanging from brass hooks, a few neighborhood spectators watching from a raised gallery
Subject: two fencers mid-bout, one lunging and one parrying, an official with a whistle, attendants carrying towels and masks, believable public-athletics energy rather than elite arena spectacle
Style/medium: photorealistic documentary still, slightly wide lens, civic sports photography
Composition/framing: horizontal wide shot, action centered but with clear foreground and gallery depth, enough negative space to show architecture and steam
Lighting/mood: hard noon skylight cut by steam haze, humid, disciplined, local, quietly dramatic
Color palette: celadon tile, cream plaster, damp charcoal, white uniforms, brass accents, muted cherry-red scoring bulbs, eucalyptus green
Materials/textures: glazed tile reflections, fogged glass, soaked cotton, worn bench wood, metal mesh masks, steam condensation
Constraints: no logos; no watermark; no modern LED arena screens; no luxury spa styling; no fantasy swordplay; keep it believable as a neighborhood league
Avoid: Olympic arena
