Prompt Extract

Use case: ads-marketing
Asset type: television commercial keyframe
Primary request: a single high-energy keyframe from an original late-1990s television commercial for Pocket Marsh Spirits, where a bedroom floor transforms into a glowing marsh game world and the handheld, cartridges, creature sprites, and toy capsules all surge into one shared scene
Scene/background: split-reality bedroom-to-fantasy transition with quilt turning into reed islands, desk lamp becoming a moon, and creature silhouettes leaping through the air
Subject: one child kneeling with the handheld in the foreground while marsh spirits arc around them as projected game-light, with the toy packaging and map inserts orbiting as secondary elements
Style/medium: polished commercial art direction with practical-light energy, original fictional franchise only
Composition/framing: wide cinematic frame, low angle, strong foreground handheld, sweeping diagonal motion, clear central focal point
Lighting/mood: electric swamp glow, firefly sparks, blue-hour bedroom shadows, exciting but not chaotic
Color palette: pond green, apricot, cream, lilac, moonlit blue, chrome accents
Materials/textures: translucent plastic, paper inserts, quilt fabric, pixel-light trails, cardboard display fragments, wetland mist
Constraints: original fictional franchise only; no real logos; no copyrighted characters; no watermark; no readable t
