Prompt Extract

Use case: atlas-contact-sheet
Asset type: theatrical wardrobe reference plate
Primary request: a dense atlas of portable desert opera costume trunks opened for inspection, showing 24 to 32 related garments, accessories, repair tools, stage props, brass claim tags, folded veils, boots, gloves, fans, scent vials, makeup tins, stitched score sleeves, and velvet rosettes arranged with rigorous spacing on a sand-toned studio floor
Scene/background: warm neutral floor or tabletop with faint grit and soft shadow, no surrounding room
Subject: the object world of a traveling wardrobe exchange for a moonlit desert opera caravan
Style/medium: editorial catalog photography with tactile realism, not luxury retail, not flat graphic design
Composition/framing: top-down or near top-down contact-sheet composition, objects fully visible, dense but readable, every item clearly separated
Lighting/mood: clean late-afternoon studio light with soft falloff and subtle dust in the air
Color palette: faded aubergine, oxidized teal, sand, brass, bone white, smoked indigo
Materials/textures: worn velvet, canvas, stitched silk, tarnished brass, cracked leather, paper labels, mirror fragments, pressed powder
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no critical readable text, no modern plastic packaging, keep the atlas tactile and disciplined rather than glamorous
Avoid: generic fashion flat lay, ecommerce polis
