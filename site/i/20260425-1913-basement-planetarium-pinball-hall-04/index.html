Prompt Extract

Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: paper ephemera suite
Primary request: photographed tabletop suite of paper score slips, maintenance tickets, constellation stamp cards, folded league schedules, coin sleeves, and short rules placards from a fictional basement planetarium pinball hall
Scene/background: cream laminate service counter beside a chrome coin changer and a corkboard edge, arranged as handled neighborhood arcade ephemera rather than polished stationery
Subject: worn paper artifacts with a coherent celestial identity system, including star-grid motifs, orbit diagrams, hand stamps, pencil scores, clipped corners, and a few short noncritical headings
Style/medium: editorial still-life photography of printed ephemera
Composition/framing: top-down tabletop layout with overlaps, folds, clips, and space between pieces so each artifact reads clearly
Lighting/mood: practical overhead light with one warm lamp pool, modest and tactile
Color palette: cream paper, midnight blue ink, pale cyan star lines, cherry red stamps, amber nicotine warmth
Materials/textures: pulp paper, pencil marks, rubber stamps, chrome clip, coin scuffs, laminate glare, worn card edges
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, short text only and not critical, must feel like local tournament paperwork not wedding invites or corporate branding
Avoid: pristine graphic design deck, luxury foil invitation stylin
