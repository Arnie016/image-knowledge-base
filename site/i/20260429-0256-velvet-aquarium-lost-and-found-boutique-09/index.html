Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: image knowledge base final PNG
Primary request: a four-panel silent comic page about a child returning the next morning to reclaim a bracelet from the velvet aquarium lost-and-found boutique
Scene/backdrop: same boutique interior across panels with front window, claim counter, tiny waiting stool, and glowing tank wall
Subject: panel 1 child outside with damp sleeve clutching a claim ticket, panel 2 clerk searching tagged drawers, panel 3 bracelet revealed on a velvet tray, panel 4 child leaving with the bracelet back on wrist and a relieved sideways glance at the window tanks
Style/medium: cinematic painted comic page, tactile ink and gouache feel, no text balloons
Composition/framing: four clear panels with varied shot sizes and strong continuity, readable gestures, no decorative borders beyond the panel structure
Lighting/mood: rainy morning blue fading into warm interior light, gentle and restrained
Color palette: teal, gray, cream, brass, muted coral accents
Materials/textures: wet coat fabric, velvet tray, paper ticket, glass reflections, drawer wood, bracelet metal
Constraints: no words, no captions, no fantasy animals, no exaggerated cartoon comedy, keep emotion subtle and observable
Avoid: children's-book sweetness overload, superhero comic action, manga effects, melodramatic crying
