Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: silent comic page
Primary request: a six-panel silent comic page about one child visiting the Public Museum of Edible Meteorites, collecting a tasting card, studying a specimen, sharing a sugar shard with a friend, climbing to the rooftop crater garden, and leaving with a folded map
Scene/background: the panels move through museum lobby, gallery case, tasting station, stair, rooftop garden, and sunny sidewalk exit
Subject: a coherent public museum world with charcoal meteorite sweets, amber sugar shards, celadon cards, orange route dots, terrazzo floors, and gentle orbit graphics
Style/medium: printed gouache-and-ink comic page with tactile paper texture and restrained museum colors
Composition/framing: six clean panels with varied camera distance, strong readable action, no speech bubbles, no captions
Lighting/mood: bright daytime, curious and communal rather than whimsical chaos
Color palette: celadon, chalk cream, charcoal, amber, muted orange, pale stone
Materials/textures: matte gouache, ink outlines, paper grain, terrazzo patterns, sugar-glass highlights
Constraints: no text, no logos, no watermark, must feel like a museum education story rather than superhero comic or children’s TV art
Avoid: exaggerated cartoon slapstick, manga speed lines, glossy digital fantasy rendering, mascot branding, dark dramatic melodrama
