Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: printed comic page
Primary request: a six-panel comic page about the stairwell championship heat of an apartment-block fermentation olympics: a resident lifts a crock from a landing, nearly spills on the stairs, regains balance with a towel, reaches the rooftop, and gets a cheer from neighbors
Scene/background: narrow apartment stairwell leading to rooftop daylight, with pennants, chalk arrows, jars, and railings recurring across panels
Subject: a small dramatic narrative inside a homemade neighborhood sport
Style/medium: printed comic page with warm inked linework and textured flat color, grounded in everyday architecture
Composition/framing: vertical page with five smaller panels and one wide rooftop payoff panel at the bottom
Lighting/mood: interior stairwell shade opening to bright rooftop sunlight
Color palette: tomato red, dill green, butter yellow, concrete gray, cream paper, amber accents
Materials/textures: ink on paper, halftone texture, painted stair walls, cloth towels, ceramic jars
Constraints: no speech balloons required, minimal or no readable text, keep continuity of props and clothing, no logos, no watermark
Avoid: manga speedlines, superhero action, grim drama, glossy digital painting, exaggerated slapstick faces
