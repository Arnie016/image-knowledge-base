Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: interior documentary photograph for a speculative hospitality environment
Primary request: a quiet cedar steam hall inside a cliffside mineral bathhouse, with long stone benches, folded indigo towels, brass lanterns, damp plaster walls, and a glimpse of a warm plunge pool through slatted cedar screens
Scene/background: interior room only, no exterior hero view, human presence minimal and secondary
Subject: the steam hall architecture and its tactile surfaces, with maybe one or two guests seated quietly or moving through steam
Style/medium: restrained editorial interior photography, believable architecture, no fantasy embellishment
Composition/framing: eye-level wide shot from one corner, strong depth into the room, foreground bench and towel stack, center kept open, no dramatic fisheye distortion
Lighting/mood: low amber lantern glow mixed with diffuse daylight leaking through screens, meditative and intimate mood
Color palette: warm cedar, limestone cream, indigo towel accents, soft brass light, pale vapor
Materials/textures: damp plaster, cedar slats, wet stone floor, folded cotton towels, brass fixtures, steam haze
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no tropical plants, no spa product display, no living-artist imitation
Avoid: luxury hotel ad gloss, empty sterile minimalism, high-tech wellness gadgets, excessive candles, yoga stu
