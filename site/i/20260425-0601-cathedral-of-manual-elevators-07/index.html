Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: silent comic page
Primary request: a six-panel comic page showing one pilgrim's ascent through the cathedral of manual elevators, from queueing in the square to receiving a stamped pass, entering the cage elevator, rising past galleries, stepping onto an upper bridge, and looking back over the city
Scene/background: all panels belong to the same alternate-history civic architecture world and reuse the same attendants, brass signs, passes, and stone interiors
Subject: the story is carried by gesture, architecture, and repeated artifacts rather than speech bubbles
Style/medium: ink-and-watercolor comic page with restrained linework and muted print color, elegant and readable
Composition/framing: one tall vertical page with six varied panels, including one large final panel at the bottom
Lighting/mood: clear daylight, solemn curiosity, quiet public ritual
Color palette: parchment, smoke blue, oxblood, limestone, black ink, brass highlights
Materials/textures: watercolor wash, printed page grain, pen line, soft shadow
Constraints: no dialogue balloons, no superhero posing, no manga tropes, no logo, no watermark
Avoid: cartoon comedy faces, dark horror, glossy digital concept-art rendering, over-dense caption text
