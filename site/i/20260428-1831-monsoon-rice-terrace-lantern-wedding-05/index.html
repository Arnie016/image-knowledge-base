Use case: environment-concept
Asset type: architectural section cutaway
Primary request: a cutaway view of a temporary bamboo wedding pavilion built beside stepped rice terraces during monsoon season, showing how ceremony, cooking, dressing, and lantern maintenance share one structure
Scene/background: terraced hillside at night with rainwater channels, planted paddies, small retaining walls, and a few distant shelter lights
Subject: sectioned bamboo pavilion with raised ceremony deck, garment-drying loft, lantern repair bench, shoe washing ledge, tea kitchen, guest mat rows, rice-bell storage, drainage gutter, and a sheltered musicians corner
Style/medium: architectural cutaway illustration with tactile realism, more like a photographed model than a sterile blueprint
Composition/framing: vertical section, structure centered, enough surrounding landscape to explain terrace slope and water flow
Lighting/mood: deep rainy night with warm internal lantern light, quiet engineered beauty
Color palette: smoked bamboo, indigo cloth, amber lantern light, wet green terraces, clay footings, cream paper accents
Materials/textures: split bamboo, tied rope lashings, woven screens, damp mats, clay basins, brass hooks, rainwater sheen
Constraints: no logos; no watermark; no futuristic materials; no giant palace scale; keep it temporary, buildable, and community-made
