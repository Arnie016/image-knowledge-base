Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: silent comic page
Primary request: a quiet one-page comic from the Sun Laundry world showing the last record sleeve of the day traveling from a courtyard clothesline to an upstairs window turntable at dusk
Scene/background: modest apartment courtyard, stairwell, balcony, and final glowing room, all from the same domestic music setting
Subject: a small sequence of panels with hands unclipping a sleeve from the clothesline, carrying it up stairs, passing a neighbor on a landing, setting the record on a portable turntable, and a final panel of the courtyard below as music drifts out
Style/medium: richly observed printed comic page with ink and slightly muted screenprint color, no speech bubbles required
Composition/framing: vertical page with six or seven panels, varied close and wide shots, one larger final panel, clear gesture storytelling
Lighting/mood: dusk moving into warm interior night, tender and reflective
Color palette: cream paper, cobalt, faded coral, pale mint, amber window light, charcoal shadows
Materials/textures: printed paper grain, matte ink, stucco walls, cotton sleeves, vinyl, stair rail paint wear
Constraints: no readable text dependence, no superhero styling, no manga speed effects, no watermark
Avoid: action-comic theatrics, cyberpunk neon, sentimentality overload, fantasy magic effects, slick digital webtoon gradien
