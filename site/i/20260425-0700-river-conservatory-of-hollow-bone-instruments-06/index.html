Test whether the branch can scale into public space while staying quiet, documentary, and materially specific.

Composition Notes

The environment should breathe, but the cases and stools in the foreground anchor the human scale.

Prompt Extract

Use case: environment-concept
Asset type: rehearsal environment scene
Primary request: a wide documentary-like scene of the river conservatory at twilight, with students rehearsing on a rooftop or terrace above reeds and water, carrying hollow bone instruments, small lanterns, folding stools, notation cards, and cloth cases while the river glows beyond
Scene/background: layered river town at dusk with rooflines, reeds, water, and a long parapet, architecture secondary to the gathering
Subject: the communal rehearsal atmosphere and how the instruments inhabit public space
Style/medium: cinematic documentary still, naturalistic, grounded, no fantasy spectacle
Composition/framing: wide frame with foreground instrument cases and stools, midground players, background water and town lights
Lighting/mood: blue-hour into lantern light, hushed, communal, tender
Color palette: indigo, warm lantern amber, shell white, weathered stone, muted river greens
Materials/textures: linen garments, bone instruments, stone parapet, woven cases, brushed brass, soft haze
Constraints: no stage spotlights, no fireworks, no giant moon symbolism, no watermark
Avoid: fantasy concert poster mood, glossy music-video styling, steampunk city, overcrowded festival scene, excessive fog
