Composition Notes

The image succeeds when the courier, scarf, ledger, and rail spaces stay consistent enough that the page reads as one escape route rather than six unrelated illustrations.

Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: silent comic page
Primary request: a six-panel silent comic page set in the world of Basalt Coast Night-Rail Heist, showing one courier stealing a ledger and escaping through the cliff railway system
Scene/background: midnight cliff stations, funicular shaft, train corridor, sea-cave tunnel, lighthouse beam, rough surf
Subject: a scarf-masked courier in dark wool slipping through a station office, taking a ledger case, dodging a guard, descending a funicular, running through a sea tunnel, and boarding a small escape rail cart
Style/medium: elegant ink-and-paint comic page with restrained palette, readable silhouettes, premium European graphic-novel feel without imitating any living artist
Composition/framing: one vertical page with six varied panels, strong panel-to-panel continuity, no speech balloons, no caption text required
Lighting/mood: moonlight and lantern glows, tense and quiet
Color palette: charcoal, storm blue, cream paper, brass, dim burgundy accents
Materials/textures: ink line, painted shadows, weathered paper grain, wet stone and metal surfaces
Constraints: silent storytelling only; keep the same courier design across panels; emphasize motion, geography, and props rather than text
Avoid: manga speed-line overload; superhero anatomy; bright comic colors; cluttered paneling; watermark
