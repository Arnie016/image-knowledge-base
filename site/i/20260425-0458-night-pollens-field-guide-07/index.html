Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: documentary macro observation scene
Primary request: a real-feeling nighttime observation table during a pollen study session, with gloved hands holding a brass loupe over a dark flower head while faint luminous dust gathers on black paper, surrounded by labeled slides, vellum envelopes, and a small notebook from the NIGHT POLLENS field guide
Scene/background: close tabletop scene at night, shallow depth of field, background falling into darkness
Subject: the hands, flower head, and drifting pollen are the focus, showing intimate micro-scale observation rather than a staged product shot
Style/medium: documentary natural-history photography, crisp but restrained, believable texture and imperfections
Composition/framing: close-up three-quarter angle, hands entering from the side, loupe and specimen centered, surrounding tools partially visible
Lighting/mood: narrow amber task light with cool blue ambient shadow, hushed and concentrated
Color palette: charcoal, indigo, cream paper, brass, muted green, pollen gold
Materials/textures: papery petals, fine dust, worn brass, glove fabric, matte notebook paper, glass slides
Text (verbatim): "NIGHT POLLENS"
Constraints: any text should be tiny and secondary on the notebook cover, no logos, no watermark, keep the biology tactile and plausible
Avoid: fantasy sparkles, horror spores, fashion edit
