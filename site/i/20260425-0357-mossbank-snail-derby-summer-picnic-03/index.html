Prompt Extract

Use case: ui-mockup
Asset type: browser-game race HUD for a whimsical garden sports game
Primary request: a playable HUD for the Mossbank Snail Derby showing a live snail race from a low side view across a moss track, with timing chips, berry-energy meter, lane ribbons, finish flag indicator, and one small map drawer while keeping most of the race scene visible
Scene/background: sunny garden racecourse with clover, pebbles, checkered leaf flags, picnic spectators blurred in the distance
Subject: the live race scene is primary, with a low-chrome themed HUD framing it in a disciplined way
Style/medium: polished browser-game UI mockup that feels shippable, tactile, and themed to handmade picnic materials rather than generic app design
Composition/framing: wide landscape game screenshot, compact HUD clusters at edges, center playfield clear, one transient prompt near lower edge
Lighting/mood: warm summer daylight, energetic but gentle
Color palette: moss green, raspberry red, cream, sky blue, butter yellow, bark brown
Materials/textures: stitched canvas panels, painted wood tabs, berry enamel icons, linen chips, leaf-shaped toggles
Text (verbatim): "FINAL HEAT" "LAP 3" "BOOST"
Constraints: keep text short and secondary, no watermark, no logos, no generic dashboard layout, preserve the race scene as the main subject
Avoid: esports overlay overload, fantasy RPG HUD, casino scoreboard
