Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: silent comic page
Primary request: a six-panel comic page showing the farewell journey of one retired weather balloon from archive drawer to final release
Scene/background: funeral salon interiors, rooftop terrace, dawn sky, and reservoir horizon across panels
Subject: panel sequence should show a brass tag being chosen from a drawer, caretakers patching a translucent balloon, a quiet procession up stairs with ribbons and reels, rooftop release into wind, a tiny balloon drifting over water, and the folded memorial program left on a stone ledge
Style/medium: elegant ink-and-watercolor comic page with realistic architecture and atmospheric weather detail
Composition/framing: vertical six-panel page with varied panel sizes and one wider release panel near the middle, no speech bubbles
Lighting/mood: cool gray dawn to pale morning light, solemn but tender
Color palette: indigo, charcoal, cream, weathered brass, faint signal orange accents
Materials/textures: textured paper, soft watercolor wash, precise ink lines, translucent balloon surfaces
Constraints: no text balloons; no logos; no watermark; avoid cartoon comedy; maintain practical observatory details
Avoid: superhero comic styling, manga speed lines, fantasy magic glow, crowded typography
