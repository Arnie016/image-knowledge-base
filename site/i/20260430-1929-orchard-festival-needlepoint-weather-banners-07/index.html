Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: final PNG for an image research knowledge base
Primary request: six-panel comic page about a young stitcher repairing a wind-torn orchard weather banner just before evening judging
Scene/background: orchard festival worktable, ridge path, and judging rail recurring across panels
Subject: panel sequence shows a sudden gust tearing one cloud banner, the young stitcher gathering thread and grommets, an older neighbor teaching a quick repair knot, both racing the repaired banner back to the rail, and the cloth glowing again in evening wind
Style/medium: warm comic illustration with tactile textile detail and clear silent storytelling
Composition/framing: one page with six readable panels, varied shot sizes, no speech balloons, no captions required
Lighting/mood: late-afternoon into blue-hour transition, intimate and encouraging
Color palette: oat cloth, indigo thread, cider gold, russet red, storm gray, lantern amber
Materials/textures: visible stitching, wool thread, banner cloth, wood tables, brass rings, apple leaves
Constraints: no logos, no watermarks, no manga speed-line overload, no superhero dramatics, no dense text
Avoid: glossy digital effects, perfect symmetry, abstract backgrounds, exaggerated slapstick, generic storybook cuteness
