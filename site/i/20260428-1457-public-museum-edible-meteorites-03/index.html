Learning Value

Check whether the theme can hold street-scale public arrival energy instead of staying inside controlled object and room views.

Composition Notes

Three-quarter framing captures facade, queue, and sidewalk life as one municipal threshold.

Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: civic museum exterior arrival scene
Primary request: the street-facing facade of the Public Museum of Edible Meteorites at bright noon, with a school group arriving, parents waiting in shade, and a modest queue under orbit-pattern canopies
Scene/background: broad city sidewalk, pale stone museum facade, round windows, banner poles, planted crater-garden planters, and a side cart selling tiny sample cups
Subject: an urban public museum whose architecture hints at geology and confection craft without becoming a theme park, using terrazzo, brushed aluminum, celadon banners, amber accent glass, and charcoal specimen motifs
Style/medium: documentary urban photography
Composition/framing: wide exterior three-quarter view showing facade, queue, banners, entry steps, and sidewalk life
Lighting/mood: hard but pleasant noon sunlight with crisp shadows and public-space energy
Color palette: pale limestone, terrazzo gray, celadon banners, amber accents, charcoal details, muted orange markers
Materials/textures: stone facade, brushed metal rails, banner fabric, sugar-glass canopy inserts, planters, warm pavement
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, text if present should be decorative and noncritical, must feel civic and approachable rather than luxury retail or amusement ride entrance
Avoid: candy-store storefront styling, giant mascots, carniva
