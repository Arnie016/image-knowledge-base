Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: ceremonial feast tabletop still life
Primary request: a richly detailed wedding feast table prepared for guests during monsoon season, built from rice dishes, herbs, lantern wax drips, and rain-ready serving tools
Scene/background: long low table inside an open-sided bamboo shelter with the storm garden softly out of focus beyond
Subject: banana-leaf platters of steamed rice cakes, herb bundles, clay bowls of tamarind broth, brass spoons, folded napkins, lantern chimneys, flower paste dishes, tea cups, pickled vegetables, rice-bell place charms, hand-tied favor bundles
Style/medium: photoreal editorial food-and-object photography, tactile and documentary rather than luxury restaurant styling
Composition/framing: three-quarter tabletop view with dense foreground detail and receding place settings, no people in frame
Lighting/mood: humid dusk light mixed with lantern glow, intimate and abundant without extravagance
Color palette: leaf green, steamed rice cream, saffron paste, clay red, tea brown, brass gold, indigo cloth accents
Materials/textures: banana leaves, matte ceramics, wet bamboo, brass, woven mats, soft steam, candle wax, hand-pressed paper
Constraints: no logos; no watermark; no modern plastic packaging; no gourmet fine-dining minimalism; keep the meal communal and handmade
Avoid: luxury hotel buffet styling; glossy magazin
