Prompt Extract

Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: museum gift-shop packaging family
Primary request: a curated family of opening-weekend souvenirs for the fictional Museum of Artificial Weather, including boxed mini cloud kits, frost crystal capsules, thunder candy tins, barometer notebooks, storm globe cartons, and small brass weather tokens displayed as a coherent packaging line
Scene/background: tabletop catalog photograph on a quiet neutral surface, no visible store interior, just the products and a few opened packages
Subject: 10 to 14 related souvenir packages and objects that feel educational and collectible rather than toy-franchise merchandise
Style/medium: premium catalog still life with tactile print realism and restrained whimsy
Composition/framing: angled tabletop family shot with a few opened boxes showing contents, readable silhouettes, no giant logo needed, short decorative text only if unavoidable
Lighting/mood: soft museum-shop lighting, calm and giftable, not flashy
Color palette: fog white, slate blue, weathered copper, muted mustard, charcoal, pale ice accents
Materials/textures: matte cardboard, foil details, brushed brass tokens, translucent domes, uncoated labels, textured notebook cloth, soft foam inserts
Constraints: no real logos, no watermark, no copyrighted characters, no toy-brand mimicry, no luxury cosmetics styling, no living-artist imitation
Avoid: candy-sto
