Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: image knowledge base final PNG
Primary request: a silent five-panel comic page about reinstalling a repaired stained-glass window in an apartment stairwell after a courtyard repair picnic
Scene/background: old apartment stairwell and courtyard at dusk with chipped walls, iron rails, laundry shadows, and warm kitchen light from doorways
Subject: neighbors carrying a wrapped window pane upstairs, unwrapping it on a landing, fitting it into a frame, one panel of colored sunset light hitting the stair wall, and a final quiet communal meal beneath the restored window
Style/medium: watercolor-and-ink comic page with tactile paper grain and restrained linework
Composition/framing: five unequal panels with clear left-to-right story flow, varied distance from close hands to wider landing view, no speech bubbles
Lighting/mood: dusk blue mixed with amber indoor light, tender but unsentimental
Color palette: smoky blue, bottle green, amber, cream paper, rust brown, charcoal linework
Materials/textures: watercolor blooms, rough paper, aged plaster, soft cloth wrapping, rippled glass
Constraints: no text captions, no superhero framing, no fantasy magic, keep the gestures ordinary and collaborative
Avoid: manga speed lines, glossy digital comic finish, melodrama, purple accents
