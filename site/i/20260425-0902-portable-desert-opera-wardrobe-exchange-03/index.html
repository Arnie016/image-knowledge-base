Prompt Extract

Use case: typographic-layout
Asset type: program suite and alteration-ticket flat lay
Primary request: a photographed editorial spread of desert opera paper ephemera from a traveling wardrobe exchange, including folded programs, short ticket stubs, brass-tag claim cards, stitched alteration notes, costume cue ribbons, wax-sealed envelopes, and swatch-mounted repair slips
Scene/background: worn wooden table or pale stone slab with soft raking light and subtle sand dust
Subject: tactile printed materials and backstage paperwork for a moonrise opera caravan
Style/medium: editorial still-life photography of real paper artifacts, not a flat vector comp
Composition/framing: top-down spread with layered but readable hierarchy, some folded edges, clips, shadows, and threads, minimal short decorative text only if incidental
Lighting/mood: warm late-day light with quiet theatrical calm
Color palette: cream paper, aubergine ink, smoke blue, brass, faded coral, sand
Materials/textures: rag paper, twill ribbon, sealing wax, metal eyelets, pencil marks, stitched fabric swatches, scuffed envelopes
Constraints: treat this as photographed ephemera, no logos, no watermark, no long readable copy, no sterile graphic-design poster look
Avoid: flat infographic treatment, wedding invitation clichés, luxury stationery glamor, clean ecommerce mockup, excessive typography
