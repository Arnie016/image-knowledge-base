Composition Notes

Continuity had to ride on the wrapped radio, shared family figures, and the shift from bench interior to rooftop dusk.

Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: silent comic page
Primary request: a vertical silent comic page about one repaired analog radio being carried through a dry-season bazaar from bench to rooftop listening at dusk
Scene/background: the same market district rendered as hand-inked and lightly washed comic panels with dusty streets, repair counters, stairwells, rooftops, and antenna silhouettes
Subject: sequential beats showing a mechanic closing the radio case, a customer receiving it wrapped in cloth, climbing a narrow stair, plugging it in beside family on the roof, and the set finally glowing to life under the evening sky
Style/medium: restrained ink-and-wash comic art with tactile paper grain, no speech balloons, no written captions needed for comprehension
Composition/framing: four stacked panels with clean rhythm, each panel composed clearly and tied by the same wrapped radio and family figures
Lighting/mood: transitions from hot late afternoon to calm dusk, modest emotional lift rather than melodrama
Color palette: cream paper, faded teal, rust brown, charcoal ink, amber radio glow, smoke blue sky
Materials/textures: watercolor wash bloom, ink lines, paper tooth, dusty masonry, cloth wrapping, wire silhouettes
Constraints: no logos; no watermark; no superhero exaggeration; no reliance on readable text; keep it local, quiet, and human
Avoid: manga speed lines; glossy d
