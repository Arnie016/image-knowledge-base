Learning Value

Test whether a ceremonial room image gains force when preparation chores, age mix, and weather traces stay visible.

Composition Notes

The prompt uses layered bench activity instead of portrait posing so the ceremony feels assembled rather than displayed.

Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: intimate preparation-room documentary still
Primary request: an indoor dressing bench scene during a monsoon wedding, where relatives paint hands, tie bells into hems, dry sandals, and sort lantern parts before the procession
Scene/background: open bamboo room with rain beyond the shutters, hanging garments, low mirrors, stacked baskets, steaming kettle, woven mats
Subject: bride seated while an elder paints marigold henna-like patterns on her hands, another relative sewing brass rice bells to a hem, children sorting blessing tags, sandals drying by the doorway, lantern pieces and thread trays on the bench
Style/medium: photoreal documentary interior photography, observed not staged, real human interaction
Composition/framing: medium-wide room view with layered activity from foreground bench to background garments, no direct posing toward camera
Lighting/mood: rainy afternoon interior with warm practical lanterns, tender concentration, soft reflections on wet floorboards
Color palette: indigo cloth, marigold saffron, wet bamboo tan, brass, terracotta, cream paper, tea brown shadows
Materials/textures: damp wood, woven mat, silk cloth, brass bells, thread spools, paper tags, steam, mirror haze
Constraints: no logos; no watermark; no beauty-campaign polish; keep the room lived-in, multigenerational, and work-focused
Avoid: salon glamou
