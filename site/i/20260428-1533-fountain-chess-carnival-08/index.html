Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: silent comic page
Primary request: a vertical comic page about a child entering the civic fountain chess carnival, studying the crowded square, playing a careful move against an elder, and walking away at dusk with a ribbon and pastry bag
Scene/background: the same fountain square recurring across panels with arcades, umbrellas, fountain tile, and tables as orientation anchors
Subject: a warm public ritual of chess, mentorship, nerves, and celebration without words
Style/medium: hand-inked and softly colored European-style comic page with tactile paper texture
Composition/framing: five varied panels with one wide payoff panel near the bottom, clear continuity, readable gestures, no speech balloons
Lighting/mood: start in bright noon and end in honeyed dusk, tender and observant rather than sentimental
Color palette: cream, teal, apricot, brass, warm stone, evening blue
Materials/textures: ink linework, watercolor wash, paper grain, tiled fountain patterns, linen clothing folds
Constraints: no text, no logos, no copyrighted characters, no watermark, continuity of fountain motif and costume palette across panels
Avoid: manga exaggeration, superhero energy, slapstick chaos, dense crowd confusion, harsh digital coloring
