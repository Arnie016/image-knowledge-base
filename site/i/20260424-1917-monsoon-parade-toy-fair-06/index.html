Prompt Extract

Use case: typographic-layout
Asset type: event poster for a fictional public festival
Primary request: a bold original poster advertising the Monsoon Parade night festival, designed as if wheat-pasted around the city, with dramatic central illustration and short decorative title text
Scene/background: vertical poster composition with layered rain clouds, lanterns, ribbon arcs, puddle reflections, and a parade gate framing the center
Subject: giant lantern koi, smiling cloud drums, umbrella dancers, frog lantern bearers, confetti rain, and a moonlit canal city suggested in silhouette
Style/medium: risograph-meets-screenprint festival poster, original graphic design, tactile ink overlap, not a modern corporate ad
Composition/framing: strong centered title block, large image mass, decorative border motifs, room for small date-strip shapes but text remains nonessential
Lighting/mood: celebratory night energy with warm lantern glow against cool rain atmosphere
Color palette: deep teal, coral red, butter yellow, indigo, cream paper, metallic silver-gray rain drops
Materials/textures: rough printed paper, slight misregistration, grainy ink, pasted-wall wear, deckled edge feel
Text (verbatim): "MONSOON PARADE"
Constraints: title text should be short and decorative only, no real sponsors, no logos, no watermark, no copyrighted characters, no direct living-artist imitation
Avoid: generic
