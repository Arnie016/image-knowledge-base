Prompt Extract

Use case: typographic-layout
Asset type: pilgrimage passport and foldout route suite
Primary request: a top-down photographed suite of printed materials for visitors to the cathedral of manual elevators, including an ascent passport booklet, a foldout district map, floor stamps, queue chits, short instruction cards, and a velvet ribbon marker
Scene/background: arranged on a pale stone table with one brass clip and one small stamp pad, treated as tactile public ephemera rather than graphic mockups
Subject: one coherent family of paper artifacts with minimal short text and strong numbering systems
Style/medium: editorial still life of printed matter, disciplined civic design, slightly worn ink and paper edges
Composition/framing: square top-down arrangement with one partially unfolded map creating spatial depth
Lighting/mood: soft daylight, archival and orderly
Color palette: parchment cream, smoke blue, oxblood, faded black, tarnished brass
Materials/textures: matte paper, letterpress ink, linen booklet cover, stamp ink, waxed ribbon
Text (verbatim): "ASCENT PASS"
Constraints: text should be minimal and non-critical, rely on numbering, arrows, seals, and layout hierarchy rather than paragraphs, no logos, no watermark
Avoid: modern transit branding, glossy brochure styling, fantasy manuscript illumination, flat design comp look
