Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: straight-on vehicle family review for a fictional traveling public caravan
Primary request: a family review of four original fictional Desert Veterinary Cinema Caravan wagons and trailers, including a vaccine wagon, a projection wagon, a water-and-feed cart, and a night-shelter trailer, all designed as buildable desert road vehicles with coherent hardware and canvas systems
Scene/background: flat hard-packed desert yard outside a low adobe depot wall, no crowd, open sky, sparse tire tracks
Style/medium: photographed vehicle catalog plate, practical municipal fleet documentation rather than luxury auto photography
Composition/framing: straight-on wide frame with the four wagons evenly spaced in one row, each fully visible and clearly distinct, minimal perspective distortion
Lighting/mood: hard but gentle late-afternoon sun with long shadows and dry clarity
Color palette: bone white, faded indigo, rust red dust, brushed brass, canvas tan, charcoal iron
Materials/textures: patched canvas roofs, wooden wheels, steel braces, enamel panels, rope lashings, dusty leather harness parts
Constraints: original fictional caravan vehicles only; no brand logos; no critical readable text; no watermark; must feel road-tested, maintainable, and humane rather than military or circus-like
Avoid: steampunk spectacle; glossy automotive ad styling; post-apocalypti
