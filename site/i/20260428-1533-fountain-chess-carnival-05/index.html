Prompt Extract

Use case: environment-concept
Asset type: architectural section illustration
Primary request: a cutaway section of the civic fountain chess carnival square showing the layered public architecture around the event, including arcades, fountain basin, pastry kiosks, balcony spectators, equipment storage, shade awnings, terrace seating, and rows of tournament tables
Scene/background: the whole stone square shown as one readable civic organism from street edge to upper balconies
Subject: architecture and event layout working together so the chess festival feels embedded in everyday city life
Style/medium: highly detailed architectural rendering with tactile realism, somewhere between section drawing and model photograph
Composition/framing: wide sectional cutaway, square centered, clean read of ground plane and upper levels, lots of small human activity
Lighting/mood: bright clear day with warm stone light and soft interior shade pockets
Color palette: pale limestone, teal tile, apricot canvas, brass, chalk white, muted greenery
Materials/textures: carved stone, glazed fountain tile, striped canvas, wood tables, linen banners, ceramic chess pieces
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no readable labels necessary, must feel plausible and human-scaled rather than fantasy megastructure
Avoid: blueprint flatness, sterile museum rendering, futuristic glass towers, amusement-park exaggera
