Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: multi-panel comic page
Primary request: a six-panel comic page from the Mossbank Snail Derby Summer Picnic showing a tense final turn, a dropped berry token, a surprise shortcut near a bridge, cheering picnic judges, a photo finish, and a tiny leaf trophy ceremony
Scene/background: community garden racecourse with moss lanes, flowers, ribbon posts, picnic blankets, and a small wooden bridge recurring across panels
Subject: the race narrative and emotional beats, with snail racers and human spectators interacting at miniature scale in a charming but clear way
Style/medium: richly inked storybook comic with watercolor texture, disciplined panel design, premium children's publishing feel without becoming saccharine
Composition/framing: six varied panels on one page, strong readable action silhouettes, one wide payoff panel for the finish, no speech bubbles required
Lighting/mood: bright summer afternoon turning golden, playful suspense and tenderness
Color palette: moss green, raspberry, butter yellow, cornflower blue, cream, bark brown
Materials/textures: ink lines, watercolor wash, paper grain, stitched pennants, shell sheen, moss texture
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no copyrighted characters, no dense dialogue, keep storytelling readable without relying on text
Avoid: manga speed-line overload, superhero action, flat webcomic min
