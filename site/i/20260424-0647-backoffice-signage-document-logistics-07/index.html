Prompt Extract

Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: environment concept frame
Primary request: a back-office archive logistics room that unifies the sign system, carts, trays, routing console, and document-transfer workflow into one believable environment
Scene/background: wide interior of a municipal archive processing room with tall shelving, work islands, cart lanes, elevator transfer alcove, hanging directional signs, and inspection tables
Subject: staffless but active-looking space full of document carts, tray stacks, sleeve racks, muted monitors, floor markers, seal stations, and shelf assignment boards
Style/medium: cinematic interior concept art grounded in real materials and public-infrastructure realism
Composition/framing: wide 16:9 frame from eye level, clear foreground worktable, midground carts and sorting islands, background shelving and transfer alcove, layered depth
Lighting/mood: early morning fluorescent and skylight mix, quiet operational atmosphere
Color palette: parchment, pine green, charcoal, brushed metal, dusty blue, muted safety orange
Materials/textures: enamel signage, powder-coated carts, worn linoleum, paper bundles, felt pads, acrylic bins, metal shelving
Quality: high
Constraints: no people, no logos, no watermarks, no readable advertising, no living-artist style imitation, keep it believable and maintenance-friendly rather than flashy sci-fi
Avoid: empty lo
