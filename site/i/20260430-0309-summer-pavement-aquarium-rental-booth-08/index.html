Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: knowledge-base visual research final PNG
Primary request: a silent six-panel comic page about one summer day with a pavement aquarium rental booth.
Scene/backdrop: neighborhood sidewalk booth, apartment stoops, refill map board, and a home windowsill return at dusk.
Subject: panel sequence showing choosing a kit, carrying the tank home, arranging a tiny temporary aquarium on a table, checking the care card in shade, returning the kit to the rinse station, and the empty wet booth closing for the evening.
Style/medium: gouache-and-ink comic page with restrained textures, clear silhouettes, and observed urban detail.
Composition/framing: six equal-ish panels with varied camera distance, clean gutters, no speech balloons, continuity of one mint bucket, one coral strap, and one child protagonist.
Lighting/mood: hot bright afternoon shifting to warm dusk, gentle and observational rather than sentimental.
Color palette: bleached mint, pool blue, coral, cream paper, gray pavement, leafy green, amber dusk.
Materials/textures: paper grain, brushy shadow edges, acrylic tank reflections, chalk signs, striped canvas, damp concrete.
Text (verbatim): no dialogue; if any signage appears, keep it tiny and secondary.
Constraints: original fictional setting, no logos, no watermark.
Avoid: manga speed-line drama, cute mascot exaggeration, glossy animation l
