Prompt Extract

Use case: environment-concept
Asset type: image knowledge base final PNG
Primary request: a temporary sun-test rack for repaired stained-glass panes installed across a shared apartment courtyard so neighbors can evaluate color and join quality before reinstallation
Scene/background: narrow multi-story courtyard with balconies, laundry lines, patched stucco, potted basil, cracked tile, and a central steel pipe frame holding many window fragments upright
Subject: repaired glass panels of different sizes casting colored rectangles onto walls, stairs, and concrete while two residents quietly inspect edges and compare shadows
Style/medium: architectural documentary concept render with believable domestic detail, not fantasy architecture
Composition/framing: slightly elevated wide view from a second-floor landing, emphasis on the rack geometry and the cascade of colored light across the whole courtyard
Lighting/mood: late-afternoon sun with sharp caustics and dusty air, calm and evaluative
Color palette: amber, bottle green, smoke blue, putty white, pale stucco beige, rust brown, denim blue
Materials/textures: oxidized pipe frame, chipped paint, wavering antique glass, taped corner guards, rope ties, rough concrete, clothespins
Constraints: no cathedral nave, no luxury gallery styling, no dense readable text, keep the building ordinary and inhabited
Avoid: magical beams, event-instal
