Use case: mixed-format

Style/medium: editorial fashion photography with theatrical restraint, believable textiles, not cosplay, not fantasy armor
Composition/framing: full-body lineup, straight-on, generous spacing, each figure fully visible from head to boot, minimal props
Lighting/mood: soft dawn studio light, calm and ceremonial
Color palette: smoked indigo, ash rose, brass, sand, soot black, pale mineral white
Materials/textures: matte silk, worn velvet trim, gauze veils, leather belts, canvas satchels, oxidized metal clasps
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no critical readable text, keep silhouettes distinct but clearly from one wardrobe system
Avoid: runway spectacle, glossy fashion campaign excess, superhero styling, steampunk gadgets, ornate crowns, generic fantasy robes
