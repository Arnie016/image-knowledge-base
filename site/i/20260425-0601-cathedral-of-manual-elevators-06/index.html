Learning Value

Test a public-machine interior that feels documentary and inhabited without turning into a spectacle shot.

Composition Notes

The elevator cage should frame the nave, not disappear into it.

Prompt Extract

Use case: historical-scene
Asset type: interior documentary elevator ride
Primary request: the inside of a brass-and-iron cage elevator crossing a high cathedral nave, with a few passengers, one attendant in uniform, visible pulleys above, stained glass and upper galleries beyond the lattice, and the sense of moving through a public sacred machine
Scene/background: interior of the elevator car with open lattice walls revealing the huge nave and suspended bridges outside
Subject: the cage elevator and its passengers are foregrounded, while the architectural void gives scale
Style/medium: realistic historical photography from an alternate civic world, subtle film grain, natural lens perspective
Composition/framing: viewed from one corner of the elevator car toward the open gate and the cathedral interior beyond, medium-wide frame
Lighting/mood: soft daylight filtered through stained glass, calm and hushed, not supernatural
Color palette: brushed brass, black iron, dusty cream stone, muted blue shadows, small oxblood accents
Materials/textures: worn brass rails, riveted iron lattice, polished wood floor, wool coats, paper passes
Constraints: no floating camera impossible angle, no horror mood, no exaggerated glowing light, no logo, no watermark
Avoid: haunted-house styling, steampunk gadget overload, action-movie tension, tourist selfie composition
