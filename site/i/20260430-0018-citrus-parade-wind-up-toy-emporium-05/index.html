Prompt Extract

Use case: environment-concept
Asset type: knowledge-base visual research final PNG
Primary request: a sectional cutaway of a fictional suburban toy emporium dedicated to wind-up citrus parade toys.
Scene/backdrop: two-story corner shop with storefront, demo aisle, repair mezzanine, stock room, confetti hopper, checkout counter, and basement storage.
Subject: the full cutaway showing customers below, a clerk demonstrating toys on a looping parade track, shelves of boxed sets, repair tools, hanging pennants, and a tiny freight lift moving stock.
Style/medium: richly observed architectural illustration with tactile model-making realism, suitable for a museum display panel or deluxe toy catalog.
Composition/framing: straight-on building section occupying the full frame, clear spatial hierarchy, enough margin for the roofline and sidewalk.
Lighting/mood: bright afternoon daylight, transparent and playful.
Color palette: cream walls, citrus brights, sky blue trim, cherry-red rails, tan wood, silver mechanics.
Materials/textures: painted plaster, varnished plywood, cardboard cartons, shiny tin rails, worn floor tiles, paper streamers.
Constraints: fictional store only, no real logos, no watermark, keep text minimal and non-essential.
Avoid: dark dollhouse gothic mood, generic shopping mall architecture, excessive explanatory labels, glossy futuristic retail.
