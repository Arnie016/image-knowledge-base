Prompt Extract

Use case: ui-mockup
Asset type: rugged field tablet interface for research corpus
Primary request: a believable expert tablet UI for an intertidal reef microclimate survey system used by marine ecologists during shoreline transects
Scene/background: the screen fills the frame with a faint hint of a waterproof bezel and wet-gloved reflections at the edges
Subject: layered tide map, sensor status chips, salinity and temperature sparklines, transect checkpoints, specimen photo queue, anomaly flags, compass heading, depth band selector, and offline sync state
Style/medium: high-fidelity product UI render, grounded and operational rather than consumer-app slick
Composition/framing: near-front view, dense but readable modular layout, clear hierarchy, multiple data modes visible at once, minimal decorative chrome
Lighting/mood: overcast coastal daylight, practical and field-ready
Color palette: fog gray, kelp green, basalt, seafoam, safety orange accents, muted cyan data highlights
Materials/textures: rugged matte glass, rubberized bezel, anti-glare surface, subtle water droplets
Quality: high
Constraints: no logos, no watermarks, no copyrighted characters, no direct living-artist imitation, no generic fintech dashboard styling
Avoid: large marketing headlines, glossy startup UI, neon sci-fi holograms, readable paragraphs, oversimplified charts
