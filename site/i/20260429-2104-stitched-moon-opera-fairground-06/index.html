Prompt Extract

Use case: environment-concept
Asset type: architectural section illustration for a speculative temporary opera venue
Primary request: a full cutaway section of a moon-opera fairground amphitheater showing bleachers, under-stage wagon storage, pulley towers, backstage dressing alcoves, lantern catwalks, ticket tents, and the tethering system for giant stitched moon inflatables
Scene/backdrop: one self-contained temporary venue on a dusty lot, ringed by poles, ropes, service wagons, and lantern posts
Subject: the venue should feel engineered and inhabited, with people visible working, waiting, dressing, climbing, and performing across the section
Style/medium: highly detailed architectural section illustration with hand-rendered realism
Composition/framing: straight-on sectional cut through the whole site, from ticket edge to backstage undercroft to audience bowl and moon rigging above
Lighting/mood: warm evening illumination inside the structure with cool dusk outside, but overall clarity for educational viewing
Color palette: bone cloth, apricot timber, indigo stage curtain, oxblood ropes, dusty earth, smoked brass
Materials/textures: canvas, timber framing, rope lashings, paper screens, patched costumes, lantern glass, packed dirt
Text (verbatim): none
Constraints: no logos; no watermark; make the temporary construction legible and practical; avoid fantasy castle architecture
