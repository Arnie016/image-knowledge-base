Learning Value

Check whether the corpus can hold a lively civic public-space scene with game activity, food commerce, and spectators without drifting into tournament spectacle.

Composition Notes

The fountain had to anchor the center while the crowd stayed legible and the foreground matches still read as real games.

Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: street festival documentary still
Primary request: the opening hour of a civic fountain chess carnival in a sunlit old-stone public square, with tournament tables circling a tiled fountain while players, families, pastry sellers, and balcony spectators gather for first matches
Scene/background: Mediterranean-style arcades, striped parasols, chalk pairing boards, pastry stalls, apartment balconies, and pale stone paving
Subject: a real annual city chess festival built from cream-and-teal ceramic pieces, brass clocks, linen jackets, ribbon badges, apricot parasols, and fountain tile
Style/medium: candid editorial photography
Composition/framing: wide eye-level scene with the fountain centered, active foreground matches, layered midground crowd, and plenty of visible public space
Lighting/mood: bright late-morning daylight, soft spring shadows, cheerful but orderly civic atmosphere
Color palette: cream, teal, apricot, brass, pale limestone, chili red, clear sky blue
Materials/textures: glazed ceramic, warm stone, striped canvas, brass, linen, chalk dust, pastry paper
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no readable text required, must feel like a real public chess holiday rather than a sports event or fantasy fair
Avoid: giant screens, neon signage, tourist-trap exaggeration, empty plaza staging, generic sports-arena energy
