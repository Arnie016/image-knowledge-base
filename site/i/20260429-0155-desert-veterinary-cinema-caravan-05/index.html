Prompt Extract

Use case: mixed-format
Asset type: handled paper and ephemera suite for a fictional traveling public caravan
Primary request: a photographed suite of Desert Veterinary Cinema Caravan paper artifacts, including vaccination passes, folded route tickets, illustrated animal-care story posters, dosage cards, lantern show handbills, species check tags, stamped feed vouchers, and a chalk-dusted ledger slip
Scene/background: spread across a dusty indigo cloth on an exam table with one tin stamp, one brass clip, and one folded blanket edge, no hands
Style/medium: photographed handled ephemera, tactile and municipal rather than graphic-design mockup
Composition/framing: angled overhead composition with overlapping but legible paper hierarchy, short decorative text allowed but not critical to understanding
Lighting/mood: warm lantern light mixed with fading desert dusk
Color palette: cream paper, faded indigo, brick red ink, soot black, brass, dusty beige
Materials/textures: creased paper, soft card stock, cloth fibers, chalk dust, stamped ink, bent clips, wool fringe
Constraints: original fictional paper system only; no logos; no watermark; image must still work even if small text is imperfect
Avoid: luxury stationery polish; tourism-poster nostalgia; clean flat vector mockup; bureaucratic wall of forms; fantasy script
Quality: high
