Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: documentary exterior photo for a speculative civic conservatory world
Primary request: blue-hour exterior view of the glass-canopy aviary on a rainy evening as the building glows from within and visitors disperse into the city
Scene/backdrop: broad public square with wet stone paving, tram stop canopy, bicycle racks, ticket lamps, and tall glazed rooflines rising behind trees
Subject: the conservatory facade and roof lanterns dominating the scene while a few stewards in weather capes escort the last guests beneath umbrellas
Style/medium: photorealistic architecture and street photography
Composition/framing: wide street-level composition with reflective foreground paving and the full building shell visible, no queue focus
Lighting/mood: rainy blue hour, interior light blooming through glass, calm civic melancholy
Color palette: mist blue, celadon, apricot glow, brass, slate, charcoal, wet green
Materials/textures: rain-dark stone, ribbed glass, painted steel, umbrella fabric, wet leaves, brass lamps
Constraints: keep it like beloved public infrastructure rather than tourist spectacle; no logos; no watermark; no fantasy skyline
Avoid: luxury hotel vibe; theme-park entrance; dystopian neon; crowded event energy
