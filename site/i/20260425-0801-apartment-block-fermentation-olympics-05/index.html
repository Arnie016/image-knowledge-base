Prompt Extract

Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: photographed paper suite for a neighborhood event
Primary request: a photographed paper suite for the apartment-block fermentation olympics including a small rulebook, balcony pennants, score chips, bracket cards, sticker seals, recipe tags, and a cloth ribbon, arranged as public ephemera rather than polished branding
Scene/background: laid out on a scratched communal table with clips and a few jars just outside the frame
Subject: the print and identity system of a homemade neighborhood tournament
Style/medium: editorial still life of civic-event paper goods, tactile and touched
Composition/framing: tabletop spread with overlapping printed pieces, one short decorative title visible reading "BRINE GAMES", other text minimal and non-critical
Lighting/mood: window light from one side
Color palette: cream paper, tomato red ink, dill green stripes, mustard pennants, dull silver clips, cobalt ribbon
Materials/textures: matte paper, cloth ribbon, sticker stock, chalk chips, steel clips
Constraints: keep the typography secondary to the artifact feeling, no logos, no watermark, no polished startup branding
Avoid: slick merch drop, pristine mockup on white, heavy text dependence, wedding invitation vibe, luxury stationery
