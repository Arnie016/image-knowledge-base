Composition Notes

The larger bottom payoff panel gives the page a clear ending while smaller upper panels keep preparation tactile and specific.

Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: silent comic page
Primary request: a six-panel silent comic page about the final hour before the Cinder Bloom cosmetics launch, showing gloved hands opening a compact, a makeup artist testing shimmer on a cheekbone, ribbons being tied around discovery boxes, a mannequin head placed in the window, a close-up of a lit mirror, and the storefront glowing at blue hour
Scene/background: all panels belong to one premium beauty-retail world and share the same materials and palette
Subject: the story should read clearly without text and focus on preparation, tactility, and anticipation rather than action spectacle
Style/medium: elegant painted comic page with realistic detail and restrained linework
Composition/framing: six varied panels with one larger payoff panel at the bottom, clear panel borders, no captions
Lighting/mood: warm interior light against blue-hour exterior, quiet and anticipatory
Color palette: black lacquer, cream, dusty apricot, copper, deep wine, moon-silver
Materials/textures: powder pigment, mirror glass, satin ribbon, embossed boxes, lacquer cases, soft skin highlights
Constraints: no text, no logos, no watermark, no superhero comic energy, no manga speed lines, must feel like premium retail storytelling
Avoid: slapstick, exaggerated expressions, comic-book halftone clutter, fashion runway drama
