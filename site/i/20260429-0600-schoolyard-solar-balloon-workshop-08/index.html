Prompt Extract

Use case: typographic-layout
Asset type: image knowledge base final PNG
Primary request: a poster wall and announcement-board study for a public-school solar balloon workshop festival
Scene/backdrop: exterior cinderblock wall near a school entrance with cork board, tape residue, and sun-faded paint
Subject: several overlapping posters, launch-day notices, stitched pennants, symbol-heavy event placards, and one large hero poster with a hand-drawn balloon silhouette, using bold shapes, arrows, circles, and only tiny noncritical text
Style/medium: photographed public poster environment, part graphic design and part documentary wall surface
Composition/framing: straight-on wall view with layered paper, tape, pushpins, curling corners, and enough spacing for a strong typographic composition without relying on readable copy
Lighting/mood: warm late-afternoon school light, civic and excited
Color palette: cream paper, sky blue, black, chalk white, orange-red, faded gold, concrete beige
Materials/textures: screenprinted ink, rough poster paper, masking tape, cork, staple holes, chalk dust, peeling paint
Constraints: use symbols and short fragments rather than readable paragraphs, no logos, no copyrighted mascots, keep it believable for a student-run school event
