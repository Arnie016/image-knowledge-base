Prompt Extract

Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: academy staff lookbook
Primary request: a six-look fashion editorial showing coordinated apprentice and instructor uniforms for a fossil-inspired nail academy in a tropical mall atrium.
Scene/backdrop: cream studio backdrop with terrazzo plinths, a few hanging palm fronds, stone sample trays, and hand-model closeups integrated into the layout.
Subject: six distinct but related uniforms with aprons, mesh sleeves, utility belts, transparent tool cases, shell-pink gloves, and manicure poses inspired by ammonite spirals, coral fans, and fossil cross-sections.
Style/medium: polished fashion lookbook photography with subtle collage structure, realistic garments and believable styling.
Composition/framing: vertical editorial plate with full-body lineup plus inset hand crops; disciplined spacing, museum-catalog energy.
Lighting/mood: soft diffused studio light, lush but controlled.
Color palette: smoky mauve, celadon, shell pink, chalk cream, amber resin, fossil gray.
Materials/textures: matte cotton, translucent vinyl, chrome hardware, polished stone samples, glossy manicure finishes.
Constraints: no celebrity likeness, no logos, no runway spectacle, no readable text required.
Avoid: cosplay exaggeration, sci-fi armor, generic spa uniforms, direct imitation of any living artist.
