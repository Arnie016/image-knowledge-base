Composition Notes

Prompt Extract

Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: cabinet side-art family
Primary request: lineup of six fictional celestial pinball cabinet side panels and control aprons from a basement planetarium pinball hall, each machine themed around a different body such as moon quarry, eclipse hotel, comet house, tide observatory, meteor market, and ring station
Scene/background: neutral workshop wall with a few hanging tools and a dusty curtain edge, arranged like a family review of real amusement machines
Subject: cabinet fronts and side panels with hand-painted star maps, chrome trim, cherry red flipper buttons, score cards, and repeatable planetarium motifs that feel handcrafted and internally branded
Style/medium: editorial product-family photography of vintage amusement hardware
Composition/framing: straight-on family lineup with equal spacing and enough cropping to emphasize side art and apron graphics rather than full-room context
Lighting/mood: soft workshop light, restrained and analytical
Color palette: navy, cream, brushed metal, amber, pale cyan, muted rust red
Materials/textures: painted wood, chrome rails, smoked acrylic, chipped enamel, laminated score cards, Bakelite buttons
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no copyrighted references, no pristine collector-showroom styling, must feel used and playable
Avoid: casino flash, RGB light strips, esports branding, glossy toy packaging,
