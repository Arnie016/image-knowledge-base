Prompt Extract

Use case: infographic-diagram
Asset type: foldout route map and building section
Primary request: a vertical foldout route map showing an apartment building cutaway for a fermentation olympics, including balconies, stair landings, rooftop judging table, basement crock room, laundry lines, tasting checkpoints, pennant markers, and small route arrows
Scene/background: treated as a photographed physical foldout pinned open on a table, with creases, tape corners, and a few clipped score tokens
Subject: how the building becomes a tournament course from basement to rooftop
Style/medium: architectural diagram and event map hybrid, tactile and browsable, not a flat software infographic
Composition/framing: tall section cutaway with clear floor hierarchy, side insets for a few detail modules, short labels only where essential
Lighting/mood: neutral indoor desk light with shallow paper shadows
Color palette: cream paper, tomato red arrows, dill green route marks, butter yellow pennants, charcoal linework, concrete grays
Materials/textures: paper grain, ink, tape, pencil marks, vellum overlays, chipboard tokens
Constraints: rely on hierarchy and icons more than text, no logos, no watermark, keep the building itself visually appealing
Avoid: generic real-estate brochure, blueprint overload, unreadable tiny text, flat classroom poster, sterile vector infographic
