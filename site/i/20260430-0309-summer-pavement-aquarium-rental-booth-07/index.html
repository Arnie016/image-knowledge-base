Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: knowledge-base visual research final PNG
Primary request: a late-afternoon return-and-rinse scene at a summer pavement aquarium rental booth, photographed like an everyday neighborhood documentary moment.
Scene/backdrop: curbside wash station under a striped canopy, shallow rinse trough, stacked returned tanks, hose spray, chalk return board, and warm apartment-street background.

Style/medium: candid editorial photography, grounded and human.
Composition/framing: medium-wide frame with foreground wet pavement and returned tanks, clear action at center, background passersby and windows softened but visible.
Lighting/mood: low warm sun with bright reflections in water, tired happy end-of-day energy.
Color palette: sun-faded mint, pale aqua, coral straps, amber light, chalk white, gray concrete, leafy green.
Materials/textures: splashy water, transparent acrylic scuffs, damp towels, rubber mats, galvanized sink edges, chalk dust.
Text (verbatim): any text should remain tiny and secondary.
Constraints: fictional neighborhood service, no logos, no watermark.
Avoid: sentimental family-ad look, aquarium fantasy, theme-park cleanliness, dramatic sunset
