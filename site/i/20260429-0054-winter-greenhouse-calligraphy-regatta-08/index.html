Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: silent comic page for a fictional winter public-sport world
Primary request: a six-panel silent comic page following a young apprentice in the greenhouse calligraphy regatta as they carry a repaired pen-oar, blot ink from a banner, step onto a small launch, ring a lane bell, watch the race begin, and smile quietly at the first painted wake
Scene/background: all panels inside and around the same humid winter greenhouse canal venue with bridges, citrus trees, drying banners, steam pipes, and frosted glass
Subject: one apprentice in practical moss-green winter workwear, older steward nearby in two panels, no speech bubbles, no text
Style/medium: tactile ink-and-watercolor comic page with disciplined panel continuity, humane and understated rather than exaggerated cartooning
Composition/framing: six clearly separated panels with varied shot sizes, clean gutters, readable actions, coherent character design across panels
Lighting/mood: pale winter daylight through greenhouse glass, warm steam haze, hopeful and calm
Color palette: moss green, burgundy, cream, ink black, fog white, cedar brown
Materials/textures: damp paper grain, ink wash, wool cloth, lacquered wood, condensation, rippled water
Constraints: original fictional characters only; no logos; no watermark; no text; maintain continuity and quiet narrative clarity
Avoid: manga clichés;
