Prompt Extract

Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: artifact atlas
Primary request: photographed atlas of celestial-themed pinball machine parts and mini cabinet fronts from a fictional basement planetarium pinball hall, including 10 to 12 distinct elements such as comet bumpers, crescent flippers, sunburst score reels, crater pop targets, brass launch plungers, constellation backglasses, orbit lane plastics, and ticket cups
Scene/background: scuffed midnight-blue pegboard wall above a repair shelf in a basement arcade workshop, arranged like working machine specimens rather than retail collectibles
Subject: many related pinball components and small cabinet fronts sharing one internal identity system, with chipped enamel, smoked acrylic, chrome trim, paper star maps, amber bulbs, and nickel coin wear
Style/medium: editorial documentary photography of mechanical amusement hardware
Composition/framing: front-on dense wall atlas, each piece readable as a distinct silhouette, organized but not sterile
Lighting/mood: warm incandescent service light with cool planetarium spill, practical and slightly nostalgic
Color palette: midnight blue, cream, brushed aluminum, cherry red buttons, pale cyan star-map accents, amber bulbs
Materials/textures: painted steel, smoked acrylic, chrome, paper score slips, Bakelite buttons, coin scratches, dusty velvet curtain edges
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no
