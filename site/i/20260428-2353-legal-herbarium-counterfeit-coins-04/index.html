Prompt Extract

Use case: mixed-format
Asset type: handled evidence-paper suite
Primary request: a photographed suite of paper artifacts from the fictional Legal Herbarium of Counterfeit Coins laid out as real handled evidence documents
Scene/background: top-down tabletop on walnut and sage felt with clipped corners and overlapping sheets
Subject: affidavits, botanical rubbing sheets, evidence envelopes, graphite coin impressions, linen ribbons, wax seals, a folded district specimen map, tabbed folio covers, small testimony cards, and one index slip
Style/medium: tactile documentary paper suite, photographed as archival ephemera rather than flat graphic design
Composition/framing: top-down spread with layered but legible hierarchy, folds, stains, pinholes, and clipped tabs visible, enough spacing to read the family relationship
Lighting/mood: soft overcast window light, restrained and administrative, not slick
Color palette: tobacco paper, smoke-blue linen tabs, sage felt, mulberry wax, graphite gray, walnut brown, oxidized silver traces
Materials/textures: rag paper, pencil marks, wax drips, cloth ribbon edges, press marks, drawer dust, brass clips
Constraints: short decorative text fragments only; no critical readable text; no logos; no watermark; must feel used in a long-running legal archive
Avoid: pristine stationery branding; modern office printouts; invitation-suite polish; luxury packa
