Prove that the branch can support a large dry landscape while keeping the communication system legible and human-scaled instead of drifting into fantasy quarry spectacle.

Composition Notes

The left foreground tower establishes operational scale immediately and lets the relay chain recede across the basin without losing the workers.

Prompt Extract

Use case: environment-concept
Asset type: inhabited landscape still for a speculative industrial world
Primary request: a wide noon view across stepped salt terraces where mirror-signal relays flash between quarry towers
Scene/background: massive white salt quarry descending in geometric cuts toward a distant basin, with catwalk towers, pulley lifts, shade canopies, and horizon markers under a dry pale sky
Subject: workers in bleached aprons calibrating mirrored frames, relay flashes bouncing from tower to tower, dust carts crossing the terraces, one severe industrial communication system in full use
Style/medium: photoreal documentary still with architectural clarity, not fantasy matte painting
Composition/framing: wide panoramic frame from a slightly elevated overlook, foreground terrace edge, midground workers and towers, deep background basin and distant relay stations
Lighting/mood: brutal noon sun, sharp shadows, shimmering dry heat, severe and procedural
Color palette: salt white, pale sky blue, oxidized brass, canvas beige, smoked black, small indigo cloth accents
Materials/textures: crystalline quarry walls, chalk dust, scratched mirror surfaces, canvas canopies, weathered timber, metal pulleys
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no lush vegetation, no romantic sunset, no giant sci-fi machinery, no crowd spectacle
Avoid: desert fantasy caravan; steampunk skyline; tour
