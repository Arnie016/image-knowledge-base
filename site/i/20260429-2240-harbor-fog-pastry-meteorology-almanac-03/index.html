Prompt Extract

Use case: typographic-layout
Asset type: printed ephemera suite for the image knowledge base
Primary request: a handled tabletop suite of pastry meteorology documents from a fog-harbor bakery, including a folded almanac, tide-dessert chart cards, weather claim slips, glazing forecast tags, launch tickets for sugar balloons, wax-sealed envelopes, and a brass token tin
Scene/backdrop: damp teak worktable under warm task light, with tiny sugar crystals, water rings, and folded striped cloth at the edges
Subject: printed public-facing bakery paperwork built from charts, wedges, pressure icons, pastry silhouettes, route stamps, and serial marks rather than readable paragraphs
Style/medium: editorial still life mixing graphic design artifact study with tactile catalog photography
Composition/framing: top-down flat lay with overlapping documents, some curled corners and rain warping, enough hierarchy to read the almanac, chart cards, and claim slips separately
Lighting/mood: intimate lamplight, cozy but disciplined, handled rather than pristine
Color palette: slate blue, butter cream, weathered paper white, copper, berry glaze red, fog gray
Materials/textures: laminated cards, wax seals, toothy paper, rubbed ink, brass tin, sugar dust, damp wood
Text (verbatim): none
Constraints: no logos; no watermark; keep text non-critical and mostly abstracted into symbols, blocks, and small marks
