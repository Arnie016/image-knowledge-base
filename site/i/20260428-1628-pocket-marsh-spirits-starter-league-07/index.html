Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: toy-store display scene
Primary request: a candid mall toy-store aisle scene centered on a Pocket Marsh Spirits endcap display, with boxed handheld kits, hanging blister packs, map inserts, and a looping demo station attracting a few children and a parent

Style/medium: photorealistic editorial retail photo
Composition/framing: medium-wide aisle shot from a slight diagonal, endcap dominant, shoppers secondary, store context visible but controlled
Lighting/mood: bright retail fluorescence with a little reflected glow from the demo unit, excited but ordinary
Color palette: seaweed green, apricot, cream, lilac, tile gray, chrome shelf hardware
Materials/textures: corrugated display board, blister plastic, polished tile, matte boxes, molded demo unit shell, metal peg hooks
Constraints: original fictional franchise only; no real logos; no copyrighted characters; no watermark; no readable text required; must feel like a plausible 1990s toy
