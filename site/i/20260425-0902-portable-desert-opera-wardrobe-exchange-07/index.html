Prompt Extract

Use case: environment-concept
Asset type: exterior establishing frame
Primary request: moonrise exterior of a portable desert opera wardrobe exchange camp at the edge of a dune station, with striped canvas pavilions, lantern paths, costume trunks unloading from pack animals, performers crossing rugs, and a softly glowing stage tent beyond
Scene/background: open desert dunes, a simple waystation wall, crescent moon, distant camp lights, no modern infrastructure
Subject: the public-facing arrival scene of a traveling opera caravan preparing for night performance
Style/medium: cinematic-but-grounded location photography or realist concept frame, not action spectacle
Composition/framing: wide establishing shot with foreground path and trunks, midground tents and figures, background dunes and moon, strong readable depth
Lighting/mood: moonrise blue hour with warm lantern pools, anticipatory and communal
Color palette: deep indigo sky, sand, ember gold, faded plum, smoke blue, bone white
Materials/textures: striped canvas, rugs, rope, dust, lantern glass, wooden crates, velvet drapery glimpses
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no fireworks, no giant architecture, keep it intimate and believable
Avoid: blockbuster spectacle, luxury glamping, sci-fi caravan tech, fantasy city skyline, harsh neon
