Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: silent comic page
Primary request: a six-panel silent comic page about a junior technician at a fictional Municipal Gelato Seismology Lab carrying a fresh gelato core through the building, comparing spoon vibrations with a railing, checking a seismograph drum, and ending on a rooftop observation deck at sunset
Scene/background: the same civic lab world recurring across panels with hall interior, stairwell, archive room, plaza edge, and rooftop
Subject: one young worker in a cream lab coat, insulated core canister, spoons, carts, charts, and small public encounters that show the branch is both practical and communal
Style/medium: printed comic page with clean inked panels and restrained color, somewhere between editorial comics and municipal educational storytelling
Composition/framing: six readable panels with varied shot sizes, strong motion from lower building rooms up to rooftop finale, no speech balloons
Lighting/mood: warm late-afternoon to sunset progression, quiet and purposeful
Color palette: cream paper, pistachio and apricot accents, berry-red trace lines, charcoal ink, warm stone, stainless gray
Materials/textures: printed paper grain, brush-ink edges, tile walls, terrazzo, freezer frost, urban stone
Constraints: no text balloons, no logos, no watermark, must preserve continuity of costume and building details across panels
