Prompt Extract

Use case: environment-concept
Asset type: architectural cutaway plate for a board-game world
Primary request: a vertical cutaway of the Beacon cliff station and the sea-cave vault beneath it from the fictional world of Basalt Coast Night-Rail Heist
Scene/background: a sheer ocean cliff at midnight, sliced open to reveal stacked railway platforms, funicular shafts, customs office, guard walkways, signal room, cargo winch chambers, hidden sea-cave tunnel, and a sealed vault chamber near crashing surf
Subject: architecture and circulation systems of the station-vault complex, designed as if for a premium game art book
Style/medium: detailed editorial cutaway illustration with believable construction logic and tactile printed-plate finish
Composition/framing: full vertical section centered in frame, cliff face and ocean visible around the cut, readable layers from top beacon to lower cave vault
Lighting/mood: moonlit exterior with warm lantern glows inside chambers, dramatic but legible
Color palette: basalt black, storm-teal sea, lantern amber, brass, weathered cream labels, muted burgundy accents
Materials/textures: wet stone, iron trusses, timber walkways, brass signal hardware, varnished rail cars, aged paper plate borders
Constraints: original fictional architecture only; prioritize spatial clarity over dense text; keep labels sparse and non-essential
Avoid: generic steampunk
