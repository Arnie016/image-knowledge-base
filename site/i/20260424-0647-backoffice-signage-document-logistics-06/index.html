Prompt Extract

Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: material behavior board
Primary request: a controlled material study of archive document trays and sleeves using one repeated tray form across many substrate variations
Scene/background: overhead calibration board on a neutral workbench
Subject: the same shallow tray, folder sleeve, and label window repeated across 12 variants including molded pulp, felt-lined aluminum, translucent resin, recycled plastic, varnished fiberboard, cork composite, brushed steel, smoked acrylic, canvas-wrapped board, rubberized polymer, ceramic-coated alloy, and paper laminate
Style/medium: tactile studio still life with research-board precision
Composition/framing: strict grid, equal object scale, repeated form for direct comparison, a few small swatch strips and hardware samples, no large labels
Lighting/mood: consistent raking light that reveals surface behavior clearly
Color palette: buff, charcoal, pine green, dusty blue, natural cork, steel gray, amber acetate
Materials/textures: emphasize edge condition, wear resistance, softness, reflectivity, grain, and translucency
Quality: high
Constraints: no logos, no watermarks, no readable text, no living-artist style imitation, keep the calibration object identical across variants
Avoid: loose collage, poster framing, fashion styling, random prop clutter, overly shiny luxury materials
