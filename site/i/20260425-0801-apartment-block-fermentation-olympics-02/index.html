Prompt Extract

Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: neighborhood competition lookbook
Primary request: a lineup portrait of six apartment residents who compete in a fermentation olympics, each wearing a distinct apron, towel sash, enamel badge, gloves, slippers or sandals, and carrying jars, crocks, clipboards, tasting spoons, or balcony pennants
Scene/background: neutral painted courtyard wall with laundry-line shadows and subtle floor markings, photographed like a community lookbook
Subject: team identities for different building floors and households
Style/medium: fashion-adjacent editorial portraiture with humor and realism, not costume drama
Composition/framing: full-body vertical lineup with even spacing, each person clearly readable, props close to the body, small shared ground shadows
Lighting/mood: soft afternoon courtyard light
Color palette: tomato red, dill green, butter yellow, indigo checks, concrete gray, white enamel, amber glass
Materials/textures: cotton aprons, terry towels, ceramic crocks, paper scorecards, metal clips, glass jars
Constraints: human and domestic, not glamorous runway, not cosplay, no logos, no watermark, keep expressions natural and neighborly
Avoid: high-fashion luxury campaign, chef whites, hipster cafe staff vibe, mascot suits, exaggerated comedy faces, restaurant branding
