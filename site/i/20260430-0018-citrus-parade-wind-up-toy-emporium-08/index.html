Composition Notes

The panel sequence had to escalate from shelf selection to rolling parade without any dialogue doing the work.

Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: knowledge-base visual research final PNG
Primary request: a six-panel silent storyboard page for a fictional toy commercial about a wind-up citrus parade set.
Scene/backdrop: toy shop shelf, checkout counter, tabletop parade route, window light, and final outdoor sidewalk reveal.
Subject: a boxed orange drum-bear toy is chosen from a shelf, wound up by a child, joins a tiny parade with balloon and horn carts, crosses a bridge, and rolls proudly to the storefront window in the final panel.
Style/medium: gouache-and-marker commercial storyboard illustration with crisp black panel borders and slightly halftone print texture.
Composition/framing: full comic page with six panels of varied shot size, readable visual progression, minimal or no dialogue text.
Lighting/mood: bright afternoon, playful momentum.
Color palette: tangerine, lemon, lime, cobalt, cream, cherry red.
Materials/textures: printed comic paper, brush texture, marker edges, halftone dots.
Constraints: silent storytelling, fictional toys only, no copyrighted characters, no watermark, keep any lettering minimal and non-essential.
Avoid: manga style, dark dramatic action, superhero poses, dense speech balloons, polished digital vector look.
