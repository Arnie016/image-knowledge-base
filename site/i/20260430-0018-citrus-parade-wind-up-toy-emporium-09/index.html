Composition Notes

Prompt Extract

Use case: typographic-layout
Asset type: knowledge-base visual research final PNG
Primary request: a bright summer mail-order catalog spread for a fictional wind-up citrus parade toy line, showing a cover-like hero page and an order-form page.
Scene/backdrop: photographed as an open staple-bound catalog on a tabletop.
Subject: one page with a hero arrangement of boxed toy sets, accessory callouts, and cheerful layout blocks; facing page with coupon boxes, small product thumbnails, reward badges, and a simple accessory matrix.
Style/medium: printed kids catalog design from the late 1970s to early 1980s, tactile and slightly worn.
Composition/framing: near top-down view of the full open spread, enough angle to show page curl and print texture.
Lighting/mood: bright natural window light, enthusiastic and commercial.
Color palette: citrus orange, lemon yellow, lime green, cobalt blue, cherry red, cream paper.
Materials/textures: offset-print paper, staple crease, halftone photos, marker circles, lightly bent corners.
Text (verbatim): any text should be short, decorative, and non-critical; prioritize blocks, coupons, numbers, and icons over paragraphs.
Constraints: fictional catalog only, no real brands, no watermark.
Avoid: sleek modern editorial minimalism, dense magazine copy, luxury typography, dark moody photography.
