Prompt Extract

Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: architectural section plate
Primary request: cutaway section of a funeral salon for retired weather balloons built into a hilltop observatory complex
Scene/background: cross-section through stone halls, drying lofts, patch library, valve archive, condolence writing room, rooftop release terrace, and a narrow elevator shaft for balloon crates
Subject: architecture is the star; caretakers are tiny scale figures; retired balloons hang like translucent ghosts in a tall preparation hall while ribbons, gauges, and launch logs are stored in disciplined bays
Style/medium: detailed architectural illustration with realistic materials, somewhere between museum section drawing and cinematic model photograph
Composition/framing: clean side cutaway spanning from underground archive rooms to open roofline and sky, readable hierarchy from foundation to terrace
Lighting/mood: overcast daylight filtering from roof lanterns, quiet institutional solemnity
Color palette: limestone, weathered slate, smoky blue, ivory latex, oxidized brass, muted rust orange
Materials/textures: stone block, timber catwalks, linen curtains, patched latex, brass railings, labeled drawers without readable text
Constraints: believable civic architecture; no fantasy palace; no steampunk excess; no logos; no watermark; no dense text callouts
Avoid: theme park cross-section, cathedral
