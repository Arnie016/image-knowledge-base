Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: final PNG for an image research knowledge base
Primary request: a fog-damp arrival promenade at a cliffside rain organ playground, where visitors in practical slickers enter a public music park built into wet stone terraces above a stormy sea
Scene/background: coastal cliff architecture with long brass rain pipes, resonant concrete chambers, shallow runoff channels, stair rails, spray haze, and distant white surf below
Subject: the first public view of the rain organ playground as a believable civic leisure space rather than a fantasy castle or a service depot
Style/medium: photorealistic documentary image
Composition/framing: wide establishing shot from slightly above eye level, promenade leading diagonally into the frame, people small in scale, no close portrait emphasis
Lighting/mood: overcast silver daylight after a shower, soft fog, wet reflections, restrained contrast
Color palette: oxidized copper, slate gray, storm blue, safety yellow slickers, chalk white foam, muted burgundy pennants
Materials/textures: rain-darkened concrete, tarnished brass pipes, rubber boots, wet stone, coarse rope, brushed steel railings
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no fantasy palace styling, no dashboard elements, no dense readable signage
