Prompt Extract

Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: final PNG for an image research knowledge base
Primary request: tabletop photograph of a souvenir prize-counter family from Brineglass Tide-Pinball Aquarium Pier, showing kid-facing packaging and redeemed objects from the same internal world
Scene/backdrop: a terrazzo prize counter with ribbed glass bins and blurred aquarium lights behind it
Subject: a family of redeemed items and packages arranged as a disciplined still life: oyster-token rolls, lighthouse candy tube, jelly-dome snow globe, embroidered shell patch cards, brass fish-spinner keychain boxes, fold-flat cardboard diorama kit, phosphor ticket strips, and a tin for tide-salt caramels
Style/medium: tactile editorial product photography, civic gift-shop rather than luxury branding
Composition/framing: medium-wide tabletop arrangement with clear groupings and some overlap, front three-quarter angle
Lighting/mood: warm indoor bulbs mixed with cool aquatic reflections, playful and shopworn
Color palette: oyster white, sea-glass teal, butter yellow, brass, coral red, smoked navy
Materials/textures: printed cardboard, enamel tins, cellophane windows, stitched patches, waxed labels, brushed metal, glazed glass
Constraints: no logos, no copyrighted characters, text minimal and noncritical, no watermarks, keep packaging plausible for a public pier gift counter
Avoid: glossy premium cosmetic
