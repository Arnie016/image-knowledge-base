Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Primary request: a silent comic page about the last ten minutes of a Rooftop Astronomy Picnic Club session before the final train home
Scene/background: same apartment rooftop across multiple panels with moonlight, blankets, thermos steam, and a dim skyline
Subject: an older volunteer and a child compare a star card, share the last pastry, pack binoculars, and wave toward the stair door as the moon slips behind a cloud
Style/medium: quiet original comic illustration with gentle physical continuity, tactile inks and restrained color, no copyrighted characters
Composition/framing: five or six varied panels with one larger closing panel, clear gestures, readable props, no speech balloons
Lighting/mood: cool moonlit night with tiny warm stairwell glow and occasional red flashlight accent
Color palette: ink blue, cream, rust red, brass, soft charcoal, oatmeal
Materials/textures: ink wash, textured paper, wool folds, enamel reflections, penciled star card marks
Constraints: no text; no watermark; original fictional people only; must feel calm, communal, and observational rather than dramatic
Avoid: manga mimicry; superhero action framing; gag-comic exaggeration; glossy animation stills
