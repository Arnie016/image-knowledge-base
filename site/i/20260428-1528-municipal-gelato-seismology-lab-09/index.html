Prompt Extract

Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: material and ingredient study board
Primary request: a tactile material board for the fictional Municipal Gelato Seismology Lab, arranging frozen ingredient swatches, terrazzo chips, glazed tile samples, graph-paper slips, spoon alloys, cone charcoal, rubber gaskets, syrup vials, and colored trace inks as one reusable visual research board
Scene/background: top-down tabletop on cream paper backing with pinned labels optional but not necessary
Subject: materials and components that define the lab's civic dessert-science identity rather than a branded retail identity
Style/medium: photographed design-material board with high tactile detail
Composition/framing: organized but not perfect grid, repeated swatches, a few overlaps, balanced density across the whole frame
Lighting/mood: bright studio daylight, crisp, analytical, quietly beautiful
Color palette: pistachio green, vanilla cream, apricot orange, berry red, charcoal black, stainless silver, faded orange
Materials/textures: sugar crystals, frozen smears, terrazzo aggregate, glazed ceramic, brushed steel, oxidized bell metal, waffle crumb, uncoated paper
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no decorative packaging, must read as browsable research material
Avoid: beauty flat-lay styling, luxury sample boards, rainbow clutter, excessive symmetry, scrapbook craft aesthetics
