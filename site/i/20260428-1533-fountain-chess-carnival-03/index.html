Prompt Extract

Use case: ads-marketing
Asset type: civic fashion lineup
Primary request: a lineup of six fountain chess carnival arbiters and players posed like a festival lookbook, each wearing coordinated linen jackets, ribbon badges, parasols or score satchels, ceramic piece cases, and soft leather shoes
Scene/background: shaded arcade in the same old-stone square with calm pale walls and faint striped awning shadows
Subject: approachable public-tournament uniforms that feel ceremonial and civic rather than sporty, luxurious, or theatrical
Style/medium: editorial fashion photography
Composition/framing: full-body lineup, even spacing, direct natural poses, enough room to clearly read garments, props, and footwear
Lighting/mood: cool arcade shade with warm reflected daylight, poised and relaxed
Color palette: cream, teal, apricot, chili red, brass, stone, dusty navy
Materials/textures: washed linen, cotton canvas, woven ribbon, glazed ceramic clasps, braided cords, brushed brass, worn leather
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no celebrity likenesses, no existing school or sports uniform cues, no readable text required
Avoid: luxury-campaign glamour, esports jerseys, military styling, black-tie formalwear, cluttered crowd background
