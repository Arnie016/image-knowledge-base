Prompt Extract

Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: pocket field guide cover set and observation kit
Primary request: a refined tabletop arrangement of a small illustrated field guide titled NIGHT POLLENS, a vellum bookmark, brass observation stamp, specimen envelopes, moon-phase checklist card, and a folded cloth sample pouch
Scene/background: dark indigo linen surface viewed top-down with generous breathing room around each artifact
Subject: the guidebook is the hero object, surrounded by coordinated educational field-kit pieces that feel collectible and practical
Style/medium: editorial product photography for a children's natural-history publisher, tactile and premium but restrained
Composition/framing: square flat lay, book slightly off center, supporting pieces fanned around it, crisp hierarchy, very legible silhouettes
Lighting/mood: warm desk lamp glow at night with soft falloff and gentle shadows
Color palette: indigo, cream, lichen green, pollen gold, faded lilac
Materials/textures: clothbound board cover, cotton paper, vellum, brass, linen tape, matte ink
Text (verbatim): "NIGHT POLLENS"
Constraints: short title text only, no logos, no watermark, no busy branding, keep it like a believable educational imprint not a luxury perfume set
Avoid: glossy cosmetics packaging, corporate brochure styling, toy-aisle plastic, astrology-kit cliches, scrapbook clutter
