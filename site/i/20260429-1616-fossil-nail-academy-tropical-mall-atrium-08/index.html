Composition Notes

The wet reflections should guide the eye into the facade while the departing students keep the image socially grounded.

Prompt Extract

Use case: ads-marketing
Asset type: storefront campaign image
Primary request: a rainy evening view outside a fossil nail academy storefront in a tropical mall atrium, with glowing display windows and students leaving after class carrying translucent tool cases.
Scene/backdrop: humid mall promenade at dusk, reflective stone floor, palms throwing shadows on glass, window displays of shell-inspired manicures and stone sample towers.
Subject: the academy facade, a few departing students and clients, luminous interior activity visible through the glass, and a memorable fossil-themed window installation.
Style/medium: cinematic commercial photography, realistic and atmospheric.
Composition/framing: medium-wide storefront angle from pedestrian height, reflections in the floor leading into the entrance.
Lighting/mood: rainy blue-hour ambience with warm interior glow and crisp wet reflections.
Color palette: sea-green and celadon glass, shell pink interior accents, amber lights, fossil gray stone, chrome, deep tropical greens.
Materials/textures: wet stone, glass, polished chrome, translucent cases, glossy manicure posters, tropical leaves.
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no critical readable text, no empty dead mall feeling.
Avoid: cyberpunk overload, generic luxury boutique, fashion-week paparazzi staging, direct imitation of any living artist.
