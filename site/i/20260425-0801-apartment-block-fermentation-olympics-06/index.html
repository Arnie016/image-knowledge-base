Composition Notes

The table should feel worked-in, with layers of scoring tools and only a breath of skyline beyond.

Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: documentary tabletop still life
Primary request: a documentary still life of the judges' tasting table during a neighborhood fermentation olympics, with open jars, small ceramic bowls, score pegs, brine glasses, enamel spoons, folded towels, handwritten tasting chips, and condensation on glass
Scene/background: rooftop judging table at the edge of an apartment block, city air and railing beyond, but the tabletop remains the subject
Subject: the tasting and scoring ritual of a domestic public competition
Style/medium: natural editorial food-and-object photography without ad polish
Composition/framing: close tabletop crop with layered depth and a hint of railing and skyline in the background
Lighting/mood: late-afternoon sun with realistic glare and soft haze
Color palette: cloudy green brine, fermented orange, cream ceramic, rust red cloth, sun-bleached concrete, amber glass
Materials/textures: ceramic, cloudy liquid, cotton towels, wood pegs, glass, enamel, chalk tags
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no lush garnish styling, keep the scene practical and slightly improvised
Avoid: food magazine glamour shot, rustic restaurant table, luxury tableware ad, excessive herbs, perfect symmetry
