Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: cinematic public-event still
Primary request: the lantern-lit finals of the civic fountain chess carnival at blue hour, with the last table isolated near the fountain while spectators fill the arcades, balconies, and steps in respectful silence
Scene/background: the same stone square now glowing with hanging lanterns, candlelit pastry stalls, shadowed archways, and the fountain reflecting deep teal water
Subject: a final match with ceramic chess pieces, brass clocks, linen-clad players, ribbon-badged arbiters, and a dense but calm crowd
Style/medium: documentary night photography with cinematic restraint
Composition/framing: medium-wide shot, final table foreground, crowd and fountain layered behind, clear sightline to the decisive move moment
Lighting/mood: blue hour with warm lantern pools, reflective water, tension and ceremony instead of spectacle
Color palette: deep teal, amber, cream, night blue, brass, stone gray, muted apricot
Materials/textures: glazed tile reflections, lantern glass, linen clothing, polished brass, worn stone, paper lantern cords
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no stage screens, no fireworks, no readable text required
Avoid: sports-broadcast look, overdone bokeh, concert lighting, fantasy magic effects, empty background areas
