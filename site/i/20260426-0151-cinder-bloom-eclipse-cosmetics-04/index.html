Prompt Extract

Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: packaging family review
Primary request: a premium packaging family for the fictional Cinder Bloom eclipse cosmetics launch, including rigid gift boxes, refill sleeves, compact cartons, sample envelopes, ribbon-tied discovery sets, tissue wraps, and sculptural inserts laid out as a coherent commercial packaging system
Scene/background: controlled studio setup on a smoked-mirror table with soft reflections
Subject: packaging only, no loose makeup products except one or two partly revealed items to show scale and use
Style/medium: luxury packaging photography
Composition/framing: three-quarter tabletop arrangement with grouped sizes and repeated closures, suitable for a design review
Lighting/mood: soft directional studio light, crisp edges, rich shadows, premium restraint
Color palette: matte black, pearl cream, muted copper foil, dusty apricot wash, deep wine ribbon, lunar silver accents
Materials/textures: embossed paper, foil stamping, lacquer board, vellum sleeves, wax-like seals, satin ribbon, molded insert trays
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, minimal or no readable text, must feel plausibly manufacturable and consistent across SKUs
Avoid: holiday gift cliches, ornate maximalism, generic jewelry boxes, cheap cardboard, bright neon cosmetics packaging
