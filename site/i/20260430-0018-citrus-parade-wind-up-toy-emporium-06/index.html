Prompt Extract

Use case: atlas-contact-sheet
Asset type: knowledge-base visual research final PNG
Primary request: a dense accessory atlas for a fictional clockwork citrus parade toy line.
Scene/backdrop: cream studio board photographed from directly above.
Subject: more than thirty related toy parts and accessories arranged in a disciplined grid: wind-up keys, spare wheels, brass axles, pennants, drums, citrus helmets, balloon baskets, confetti carts, trumpets, repair tins, sticker sheets, tiny bridges, ticket stubs, and folded instruction mini-cards.
Style/medium: tactile catalog contact-sheet photography with slightly imperfect hand-arranged spacing.
Composition/framing: strict top-down view, full grid filling the frame edge to edge with consistent shadows and clear object separation.
Lighting/mood: bright diffused studio light, informative and playful.
Color palette: citrus brights tempered by cream paper, tin silver, brass, cobalt, and red.
Materials/textures: scuffed enamel paint, brushed metal, paper stickers, thin cardboard, molded plastic, miniature cloth.
Constraints: fictional accessories only, no logos, no watermark, keep any text tiny and non-essential.
Avoid: sterile minimalist product photography, monochrome palette, luxury watch layout, empty negative space, dark background.
