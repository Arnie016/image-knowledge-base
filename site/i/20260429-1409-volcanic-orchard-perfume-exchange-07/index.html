Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: cinematic narrative frame from a speculative fragrance world
Primary request: a night transfer of volatile perfume crates from the volcanic orchard exchange onto a small tram descending through ash mist toward the port
Scene/backdrop: wet basalt loading platform cut into the hillside, narrow tram cars, distant orchard terraces glowing faintly above, vapor venting from cooling crates into blue-black rain air
Subject: a few workers in layered coats guiding crate trolleys, checking bottle seals, and lifting one crate into the tram while a sampler holds a lantern and blotter case
Style/medium: cinematic still with photorealistic detail, somewhere between film production design and documentary drama, not fantasy battle art
Composition/framing: wide horizontal frame with the tram angled into depth, active foreground crate transfer, misty receding tracks, and a strong diagonal of light
Lighting/mood: cold rainy night with warm lamp pools, mineral fog, reflective ground, tense but quiet atmosphere
Color palette: blue-black, ember amber, olive black, sulfur cream highlights, rusted copper, wet stone gray
Materials/textures: rain-slick rails, canvas coats, wax seals, frosted crate lids, wet stone, lantern glass, steam, wood slats
Constraints: original setting only; no logos; no watermark; no readable signage dependency; keep it intimate and logist
