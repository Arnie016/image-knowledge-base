Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: silent four-panel comic page for a children's science guide
Primary request: a four-panel comic page showing a young night naturalist following a drifting trail of luminous pollen from a windowsill flower, through a dark hallway, up a stairwell, and onto a moonlit rooftop garden where the air is full of floating grains
Scene/background: each panel is part of one continuous home-and-rooftop environment at night
Subject: quiet observation and discovery, with the pollen trail linking the panels and carrying the story without speech bubbles
Style/medium: printed comic illustration with gouache-and-ink texture, natural-history detail, restrained storytelling
Composition/framing: one square page containing four uneven panels with a strong final wide rooftop panel, readable silhouettes, minimal captions if any
Lighting/mood: dim domestic night light moving toward open moonlight, tender and observant rather than adventurous spectacle
Color palette: indigo, cream, muted sage, dusty lilac, pollen gold highlights
Materials/textures: matte print ink, paper grain, brushed gouache shadows, soft pencil contour
Text (verbatim): "NIGHT POLLENS"
Constraints: no speech bubbles, no dense text, if any title appears keep it tiny and secondary, no watermark, no copyrighted characters
Avoid: superhero action energy, manga speed lines, glossy digital fantasy art
