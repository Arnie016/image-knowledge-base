Prompt Extract

Use case: mixed-format
Asset type: architectural section illustration for a museum building
Primary request: a believable section cutaway of the fictional Museum of Artificial Weather, revealing a rain theater, mist gallery, frost archive, thunder balcony, school workshop mezzanine, rooftop vane garden, and entry hall in one coherent public building
Scene/background: full-building side section with a little surrounding city context only if needed, presented like a printed architectural plate rather than a theme-park concept painting
Subject: one original civic museum building with clear visitor circulation, layered weather-themed rooms, and repeated material language
Style/medium: meticulous architectural illustration with tactile realism, halfway between a section drawing and a photographed maquette
Composition/framing: wide horizontal cutaway with readable stacked spaces and tiny human silhouettes for scale only if useful, no dense labeling required
Lighting/mood: dusk outside with warm interior galleries glowing softly, curious and public rather than luxurious
Color palette: slate stone, pale concrete, weathered copper, cloud white, deep blue-gray, muted amber light
Materials/textures: terrazzo floors, brushed brass rails, frosted glass chambers, painted steel frames, poured concrete, translucent roof panels
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no fantasy castle exaggeration
