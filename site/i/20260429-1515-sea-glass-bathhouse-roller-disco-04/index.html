Prompt Extract

Use case: atlas-contact-sheet
Asset type: gear atlas for a speculative public roller disco
Primary request: a dense top-down contact sheet of rental skates, polished wheels, striped knee socks, shell badges, skate keys, repair tins, ticket punches, mesh gloves, wristbands, citron soda bottles, elbow pads, organ-booth headphones, and striped scarves from the sea-glass bathhouse roller disco
Scene/backdrop: neutral cream tabletop with faint guide lines and small shadow separation between objects
Subject: 20 to 30 clearly distinct handled objects arranged in tidy clusters, each object from the same visual world and varying by wear, color, size, and function
Style/medium: editorial catalog photography from directly overhead
Composition/framing: dense but legible gridless arrangement filling the frame edge to edge, generous enough spacing to keep silhouettes clear
Lighting/mood: soft museum-like daylight with clean shadows and crisp surface detail
Color palette: aqua, coral, cream, chrome, citron, navy, pale peach, terrazzo flecks
Materials/textures: suede boot uppers, scuffed rubber wheels, frosted plastic bottles, punched paper, satin ribbon, mesh fabric, chrome tools, terry cloth
Constraints: no logos; no watermark; no need for readable labels; keep it grounded in public leisure equipment rather than luxury collectibles
Avoid: toy-franchise packaging; fantasy trinkets; black void
