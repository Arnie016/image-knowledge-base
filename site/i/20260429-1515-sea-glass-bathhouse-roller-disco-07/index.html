Composition Notes

Prompt Extract

Use case: environment-concept
Asset type: architectural cutaway plate for a speculative public recreation building
Primary request: a vertical cutaway section of the sea-glass bathhouse roller disco, showing the terrazzo rink basin, balcony galleries, locker rooms, rental desk, repair alcove, organ loft, skylight roof, and shallow water channels beneath the circulation edges
Scene/backdrop: clean neutral background as if mounted in a museum architecture exhibition
Subject: a single building section with enough tiny human figures to explain scale and activity, including skaters, spectators, ushers, mechanics, and soda-bar workers
Style/medium: detailed architectural illustration with physical texture and subtle hand-rendered discipline
Composition/framing: full building section centered on the page, from roof lanterns to foundation, no perspective distortion
Lighting/mood: bright daytime light washing through skylights and revealing tile depth, warm and communal rather than dramatic
Color palette: cream stone, sea-glass aqua, coral rails, checkerboard navy-and-cream lockers, pale terrazzo, citron notes
Materials/textures: glazed tile, glass block, terrazzo, chrome railings, frosted glazing, painted steel, wet stone edges
Constraints: original building logic; no logos; no watermark; keep the section legible and architectural, not fantasy-palace ornamental
Avoid: exploded spaceshi
