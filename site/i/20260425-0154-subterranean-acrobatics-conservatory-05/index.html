Prompt Extract

Use case: mixed-format
Asset type: architectural section plate for a fictional arts institution
Primary request: a believable cutaway section of the Subterranean Acrobatics Conservatory carved into pale limestone, revealing a training ring, rigging lofts, costume workshop, chalk room, dormitory niches, narrow audience gallery, and elevator arrival chamber in one coherent underground building
Scene/background: sectional drawing rendered as a richly detailed architectural image on a neutral paper field, no city skyline, focus on the building interior and rock mass
Subject: layered underground rehearsal architecture with original acrobatics spaces, ladders, ropes, hoists, storage alcoves, lantern rails, and small human figures for scale
Style/medium: hybrid of architectural visualization and crafted section plate, physically believable, not fantasy dungeon concept art
Composition/framing: wide cross-section with clear vertical stacking, readable circulation paths, one dominant ring chamber at center, restrained annotations only if decorative
Lighting/mood: warm rehearsal light inside against cool stone mass, studious and mysterious rather than ominous
Color palette: limestone beige, soot, oxblood accents, muted cobalt wayfinding marks, brass, warm lantern amber
Materials/textures: cut stone, lime plaster, timber catwalks, brass railings, canvas curtains, chalky floors, paper grain
