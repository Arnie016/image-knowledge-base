Prompt Extract

Use case: environment-concept
Asset type: architectural section cutaway
Primary request: a section cutaway of a three-story analog radio repair house embedded in a dry-season bazaar block
Scene/background: dense old market street with neighboring shopfronts, rooftop water tanks, improvised shade cloth, and dusty sky
Subject: ground-floor customer counter and quick-fix benches, second-floor parts cage and coil rewinding tables, third-floor quiet tuning room, staircase, tiny tea corner, hanging antennas, storage drawers, fan belts, and one rooftop mast platform
Style/medium: tactile architectural cutaway illustration closer to a photographed physical model than sterile CAD
Composition/framing: vertical slice through the building with enough street context to show bazaar density and the relation between stall, workshop, and roof
Lighting/mood: late afternoon heat with warm side light and cooler interior shadows, deeply buildable and human
Color palette: chalk masonry, faded teal shutters, copper wire, dull steel, parchment paper, smoke gray, rust dust
Materials/textures: chipped plaster, wood shelves, wire bundles, canvas shades, metal stairs, glass dial windows, paper tags
Constraints: no logos; no watermark; no futuristic machinery; no giant factory scale; keep the building modest, improvised, and plausible
