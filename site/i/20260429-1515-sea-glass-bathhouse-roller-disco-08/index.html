Prompt Extract

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: narrative key art for a speculative public roller disco world
Primary request: a dramatic but warm closing-lap scene in the sea-glass bathhouse roller disco where two skaters lean into the final turn, a referee signals the lap count, balcony spectators wave striped scarves, and the organ loft glows above
Scene/backdrop: the bathhouse rink interior at late golden hour, skylights still bright, coral rails and aqua tile catching the light
Subject: the leading skaters in the foreground, one in coral and one in navy, with the crowd, organist, and soda runner visible as supporting story details
Style/medium: richly composed illustrated poster art with crisp shapes and tactile surface grain, somewhere between editorial illustration and classic event key art
Composition/framing: dynamic wide composition from low rink level, curving track lines pulling the eye through the turn toward the organ loft
Lighting/mood: celebratory, kinetic, and affectionate, with warm sun bands and clean shadows
Color palette: sea-glass aqua, coral, cream, navy, citron highlights, soft amber sunlight
Materials/textures: screenprint-like grain, matte ink surfaces, polished terrazzo sparkle, satin fabric sheen kept subtle
Constraints: original characters only; no logos; no watermark; keep the feeling communal and sportsmanlike rather than violent competition
Avoid: super
