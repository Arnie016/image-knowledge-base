Prompt Extract

Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: exterior environment concept for a fictional public venue
Primary request: the exterior arrival view of the Glass Orchard Dessert Casino, a grand but approachable greenhouse palace with citrus trees on terraces, glass lantern roofs, warm pastry counters visible inside, and guests arriving for evening tasting rounds
Scene/background: exterior plaza and facade only, with a hint of city garden surroundings and reflective paving after a light washdown
Subject: the building as the hero, people secondary, visible entrance sequence, outdoor seating niches, orchard terraces, brass details, and layered glasshouse volumes
Style/medium: realistic environment rendering with architectural clarity and lived-in public-space detail
Composition/framing: slightly low wide angle from the arrival court, strong central entrance, side terraces, readable skyline, no aerial view
Lighting/mood: soft twilight blue mixed with warm interior glow, welcoming and luminous rather than flashy
Color palette: pale stone, sage metalwork, amber interior light, citrus orange accents, blush sky reflections
Materials/textures: ribbed glass, polished brass, wet stone paving, planted citrus trees, linen awnings, glazed tile glimpses
Constraints: no logos, no watermark, no slot-machine signage, no giant billboards, no living-artist imitation
