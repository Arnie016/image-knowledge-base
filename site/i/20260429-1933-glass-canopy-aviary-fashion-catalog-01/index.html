Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: documentary interior photo for a speculative public aviary catalog
Primary request: candid wide interior photo of a glass-canopy urban aviary conservatory during morning visiting hours
Scene/backdrop: a long vaulted greenhouse hall with curved steel ribs, suspended roost ladders, shallow mist channels, seed kiosks, hanging route flags, and visitors moving quietly below free-flying small birds
Subject: migratory stewards in pale weather capes and clip-on scorecards guiding families through the promenade while birds cross shafts of light overhead
Style/medium: photorealistic documentary architecture and public-life photography
Composition/framing: wide eye-level view from one side of the promenade, clear depth through foreground railing, midground visitors, and distant canopy bridges
Lighting/mood: sunlit humid morning, luminous glass reflections, calm lush atmosphere
Color palette: celery green, apricot, fog white, brass, slate, soft teal
Materials/textures: wet tile, ribbed glass, brushed brass tags, waxed canvas satchels, silk capes, moss planters
Constraints: original public-building world only; no logos; no watermark; avoid theme-park spectacle; keep it civic and buildable
Avoid: tropical resort luxury; fantasy palace; zoo signage clutter; nightclub drama
