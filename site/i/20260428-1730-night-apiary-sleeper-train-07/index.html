Prompt Extract

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: documentary station transfer still
Primary request: workers transferring insulated hive cabinets from the original fictional Night Apiary Sleeper Train onto a small snowy platform during a midnight siding stop
Scene/background: remote mountain station with one dim signal lamp, snow-packed platform boards, freight sled, dark pines, and the warm train windows stretching into fog
Subject: three or four bundled adults in practical gloves and aprons carefully moving boxy amber-lit hive cabinets with straps and hand trucks; no faces close to camera
Style/medium: believable photojournalistic still, natural human posture, no action-movie drama
Composition/framing: medium-wide eye-level frame, transfer action in foreground, train flank and disappearing cars behind, snow texture clearly visible
Lighting/mood: blue-black winter night with amber window light and one sodium station lamp, focused and hushed
Color palette: deep navy, amber, dirty snow white, dark green pine, rusted metal, cream canvas
Materials/textures: packed snow, wet timber, metal hand truck, waxed canvas straps, frosted glass, heavy wool
Constraints: original fictional rail operation only; no logos; no watermark; no swarming insects; adults only; must feel careful and procedural but not like a support dashboard
Avoid: emergency-disaster vibe; heroic posing; sci-fi cargo crates
