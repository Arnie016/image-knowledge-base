Prompt Extract

Use case: infographic-diagram
Asset type: image knowledge base final PNG
Primary request: a tidal provenance map explaining where recovered pocket objects in the aquarium district tend to surface before reaching the lost-and-found boutique
Scene/backdrop: elegant wall map or printed diagram pinned flat, combining canal edges, public baths, tram stops, sea wall stairs, promenade benches, arcade entrances, and sorting routes
Subject: a geographic storytelling map with currents, foot traffic zones, locker districts, rain runoff arrows, and clusters of recovered items represented by icons like keys, combs, watches, shell charms, and bracelets
Style/medium: refined cartographic diagram, tactile printed artifact rather than digital dashboard
Composition/framing: full map in frame, strong hierarchy, some legend blocks and inset diagrams, readable as a poster even if text stays minimal
Lighting/mood: cool museum-study light, bureaucratic and slightly romantic
Color palette: faded teal, cream, charcoal, brass, sea-glass green, pale coral
Materials/textures: printed paper, pencil marks, tape corners, route circles, translucent overlays, pin shadows
Constraints: minimal text only, no logos, no tourist map kitsch, no dense modern UI chrome
Avoid: fantasy treasure map tropes, glossy transit app look, pirate iconography, bright primary colors
