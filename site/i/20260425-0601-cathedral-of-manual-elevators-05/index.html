Composition Notes

Prompt Extract

Use case: environment-concept
Asset type: city-scale exterior documentary frame
Primary request: a public square outside the cathedral of manual elevators at noon, with people arriving, queueing under velvet ropes, attendants managing brass gate entries, stone facades, ticket kiosks, hanging floor signs, and the elevator towers rising above the district
Scene/background: broad civic plaza connected to surrounding streets and nearby shops, dry weather, clear air
Subject: the architecture and public choreography are the main subject, not a single hero person
Style/medium: historical editorial photography in a believable alternate 1920s city, naturalistic but highly composed
Composition/framing: wide street-level frame with foreground queue markers, midground entrance steps, and tower mass in the background
Lighting/mood: hard but elegant midday sun with crisp shadows, public and orderly
Color palette: pale stone, slate blue, oxblood accents, dark uniforms, brass highlights
Materials/textures: limestone paving, iron gates, paper tickets, polished brass, wool coats, glass kiosk windows
Constraints: no cars dominating the frame, no fantasy smoke, no modern signage, no logo, no watermark
Avoid: theme-park crowding, wedding-photo posing, tourist snapshot feel, dark gothic horror
