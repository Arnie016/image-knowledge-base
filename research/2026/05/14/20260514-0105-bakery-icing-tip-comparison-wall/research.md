## What Each Image Should Teach

- `Piping Tip Names And Uses Chart`: match common tip shapes to the textures they produce
- `Buttercream Stiffness Comparison Guide`: show how soft, medium, and stiff frosting behave on cakes and cupcakes
- `Star Tip Versus Round Tip Border Board`: compare shell, rope, bead, pearl, and scallop edges from different nozzles
- `Buttercream Flower Tip Taxonomy`: connect petal, leaf, drop-flower, and ruffle tips to visible flower forms
- `How To Fill A Piping Bag Poster`: explain coupler setup, bag folding, filling, twisting, and pressure control
- `Cupcake Swirl And Rosette Guide`: compare recognizable swirl finishes used in bakery counters and class demos
- `Cake Decorating Tools Tray Reference`: identify the core decorating kit without drifting into generic baking clutter
- `Frosting Trouble Signs Training Wall`: show drooping rosettes, ragged stars, air bubbles, splitting buttercream, and overmixed color
- `Pastry School Decorating Station`: place the posters, tools, and cake turntables in a believable training room
- `Cake Finishing Bench Section Cutaway`: reveal how a bakery finishing bench organizes cakes, bags, racks, and wash bins
