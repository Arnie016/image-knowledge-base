## What Each Image Should Teach

- `Lantern Kite Gecko Turnaround Sheet`: lock front, side, back, and three-quarter body proportions with recurring satchel, cape patch, ribbon tail, and spool placement
- `Lantern Kite Gecko Expression And Reaction Pack`: show acting range from alert crouch to frustrated repair to relieved kite return without changing face or costume logic
- `Rooftop Gecko Species Dossier Plate`: explain anatomy, scale, grip logic, tail ribbon function, and repair-tool relation as a reusable species reference
- `Kite Repair Satchel And Costume Detail Plate`: isolate costume anchors and repair tools so prop and wardrobe continuity survive across shots
- `Rooftop Chase Storyboard Beat Page`: map the key beats of the rooftop chase and recovery sequence as a readable six-panel production page
- `Bell Tower Rooftop Camera Blocking Board`: show shot size, angle, subject relation, and camera path across the tea-house roof and bell-tower ledge
- `Kite Catch Animation Timing Ladder`: show a recurring action loop with pose spacing, anticipation, airtime, catch, and landing recovery
- `Dusk Rooftop Continuity Environment Sheet`: fix architecture, prop positions, lantern strings, gutters, and skyline landmarks for layout continuity
- `Seedance To CapCut Edit Planning Board`: connect still-image beat IDs to motion passes, camera moves, and cut timing for downstream editing
- `Lantern Kite Recovery Cinematic Proof Frame`: prove the full asset language works in-scene as a finished still rather than only as boards
