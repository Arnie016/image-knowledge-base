## What Each Image Should Teach

- `Healthy Orchid Roots Versus Root Rot Chart`: show the color, firmness, translucency, and velamen condition differences between healthy roots and rotting roots
- `Bark Mix Versus Sphagnum Moss Orchid Guide`: compare common orchid potting media through airflow, water retention, and visible root behavior
- `Orchid Pot Anatomy And Drainage Plate`: expose the clear pot, side slots, drainage holes, cachepot, label stake, and root spread in one readable plate
- `How To Repot A Phalaenopsis Orchid Poster`: show the step sequence from unpotting and trimming to media refill, stake reset, and first watering pause
- `Orchid Watering Signs Comparison Guide`: contrast thirsty roots, overwatered roots, wrinkled leaves, plump leaves, dry bark, and soaked moss
- `Aerial Root Versus Dead Root Visual Guide`: teach the difference between normal exposed aerial roots and roots that should be trimmed
- `Orchid Bench Tool Kit Layout`: identify the basic rescue-bench kit for repotting and root checks
- `Clear Nursery Pot Root Zone Cutaway`: prove how roots, bark, moss pockets, and drainage channels stack spatially inside a common orchid pot
- `Greenhouse Orchid Triage Lesson Wall`: show how a real training wall or rescue bench displays charts, tagged plants, and root examples for staff
- `Orchid Quarantine Shelf Section Cutaway`: show shelf spacing, airflow, tagged isolation pots, catch trays, and observation cards in one buildable section
