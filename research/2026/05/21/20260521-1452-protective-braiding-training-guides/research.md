- `Box Braids And Knotless Braids Chart`: compare root start, tension feel, silhouette, and installation logic
- `Cornrow Parting Pattern Guide`: show practical scalp maps and the geometry behind straight, curved, and feed-in rows
- `Braid Size Section Grid Poster`: link section-square size to finished braid thickness
- `Feed In Braid Sequence Poster`: show the recurring action logic of adding hair cleanly through a row
- `Tight Braid Warning Signs Chart`: distinguish healthy installation from traction-risk clues
- `Braiding Tool Tray Reference`: identify the compact tools that actually matter at the station
- `Extension Fiber Blend Swatch Board`: compare fiber texture, finish, and color-blend planning
- `Bead Cuff And Endings Plate`: show finishing hardware and how braid ends are secured
- `Cosmetology Braiding Station Setup`: prove how the charts and tools live together in a real training station
- `Braiding Clinic Section Cutaway`: show mirror, chair, practice-head racks, trolley, sanitation shelf, and poster wall as a buildable teaching room
